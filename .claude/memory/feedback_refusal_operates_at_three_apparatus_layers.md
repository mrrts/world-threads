**Rule.** Apparatus-honest refusal operates at three structurally-distinct layers, each with its own discipline + apparatus + worked-example. Recognizing the layer at which a refusal operates is part of the discipline; conflating layers risks treating refusal as undifferentiated "did not fire" when each refusal-class is doing different load-bearing work.

| Layer | What gets refused | Apparatus that enforces | Worked example |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Firing-decision** | A specific Sapphire-firing decision after evidence is in | 5-clause firing-audit + codex preemptive consult + founding-author signature | Crown 15 Atonement HOLD-pending-sharper-falsifier (2026-05-08 ~05:00; codex named path; founding-author honored) |
| **Candidacy-closure** | The candidacy itself, on its current claim-form, with hard-severance language for any future revival | Move-N codex reframe consult + hard-severance markers + read-this-first directive | Crown 17 kavod-pattern Transfiguration REFUSED on rendering-axis (2026-05-07; codex Move 9 verdict; 5 severance requirements) |
| **Scope-decision** | A candidacy proposal at template-evaluation time, before any arc execution | Pre-flight Move-1 template with structural-risk acknowledgment + dual-path presentation (Path A refuse-at-eval $0 OR Path B pursue) | Eucharist Path A available per Move-1 template (2026-05-08 ~00:30; predicted ~60% REFUSE; structurally weakest of central-content tracker) |

**Why these three are structurally distinct.**

- **Firing-decision-layer refusal** preserves tier-meaning for downstream decisions (what doctrine ships / what reaches users / what gets cited). It says: "the evidence is in; the threshold is the threshold; this candidacy doesn't reach it." Crown 15 Atonement HOLD lives here. The candidacy stays open at claim-tier with codex's path-to-FIRE named for future-arc maturation.
- **Candidacy-closure-layer refusal** ends the current claim-form with hard-severance. It says: "this candidacy as currently framed cannot be salvaged on its current evidence; any future arc on similar territory must be a fully-new candidacy with severance markers explicit." Crown 17 kavod-pattern lives here. The candidacy CANNOT be silently reopened; rebooting requires explicit hard-severance Move-1.
- **Scope-decision-layer refusal** prevents arc execution from beginning. It says: "this candidacy's claim-shape is structurally inadvisable for our methodology; the apparatus chooses to refuse at template-evaluation time as itself load-bearing scope-edge characterization." Eucharist Path A lives here. The methodology grows by what it refuses to attempt; the refusal IS evidence about where the methodology's reach ends.

**Anchor:** *"the methodology grows by what it refuses to attempt"* (lifted verbatim from Eucharist Move-1 template; phrase generalizes across all three layers).

**Why this matters more than crown-counting.** Crown 16 Ascension's Sapphire-firing claim is that **shell-substrate hooks preserve calibrated disciplines via external block/redirect**. THIS observation extends Crown 16's claim — the same structural-enforcement-tier-promoted disciplines also operate at candidacy-closure and at scope-decision. The apparatus's earn/refuse symmetric calibration isn't only a firing-decision-layer phenomenon; it operates at every layer where a candidacy can be born, advanced, or closed. The methodology earns by what it fires AND grows by what it refuses to attempt.

**How to apply.**

- **At firing-decision time:** the 5-clause firing-audit + codex consult + founding-author signature path is the established discipline (Crown 13/14/15/16 precedent). When the audit verifies + codex blesses, FIRE. When the audit gaps OR codex HOLD-pending or REFUSE, do NOT fire on apparatus authority alone. The discipline is symmetric; what fires is what's earned.
- **At candidacy-closure time:** when a candidacy has run sufficient evidence to reach REFUSE verdict, lock the REFUSED state with hard-severance language preventing silent revival. Crown 17 kavod-pattern Move 11 commit (`e8a16155`) is the canonical worked-example. The 5 severance requirements (acknowledgment of failure / new frozen claim / new witness ladder / new success criteria / different crown-name candidate) become the read-this-first directive for any future arc on the territory.
- **At scope-decision time:** when pre-authoring a Move-1 template AND structural-risk is high (e.g., codex predicted-HOLD on perspectives-presentation drift; ecclesiastical pluralism makes standard probe pattern inapplicable; central-content axis with substrate-distinctness compression), include DUAL-PATH presentation (Path A refuse-at-template-eval $0 OR Path B pursue with substantially-narrower-than-others claim). Path A is itself a load-bearing apparatus-honest move characterizing the methodology's scope-edge. Eucharist Move-1 template is the canonical worked-example.

**Refusal carve-outs (refusing AT each layer is bounded by these, not blocked by them):**

- Refusal AT firing-decision-layer requires evidence-is-in (5-clause audit completable). Premature refusal before evidence is in is HOLD-pending-X, not REFUSE.
- Refusal AT candidacy-closure-layer requires sufficient empirical falsification (e.g., kavod arc Moves 6-7 smoke tests revealing pre-baseline-resident behavior). Closure on speculation alone is dry-well-exit not REFUSE.
- Refusal AT scope-decision-layer requires structural-risk ARGUMENT articulated in the template. Refuse-without-articulating-why-the-claim-is-structurally-weak risks aimless deferral; the articulation IS the evidence.
