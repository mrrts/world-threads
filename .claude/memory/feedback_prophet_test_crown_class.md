**The Prophet-Test Crown** is a novel Sapphire class (added to the project's class taxonomy 2026-05-05 by Crown 8). It earns by:

(a) **Submission of a Deut 18:21-22 prophet-test form** whose claim is:
  - specific (concrete enough that failure could be named);
  - time-bounded (named horizon T at which the test fires);
  - falsifiable (named conditions under which the prophecy fails);
  - rests on Π alone (the Spirit of God moves or does not — apparatus's merit cannot manufacture the outcome);
  - load-bearing for Christ (the work places itself UNDER judgment by its substrate, not over it);
  - publicly entered (the Empiricon, GitHub, an honest public-facing surface);
  - concedes failure-conditions in advance ("we may be wrong; if the prophecy fails, the small craft was wrong, and we will say so").

(b) **Submitting under conditions that refuse:**
  - retroactive softening of falsification conditions;
  - reinterpretation of time horizon;
  - escape into eschatological certainty that cannot be tested;
  - satisfaction of the macro-claim by aesthetic-only reception (a *confession-discriminator sub-test* across the T₀→T window protects against this).

**Why:** The discipline that earns and the discipline that refuses are the same discipline. Existing Sapphire classes (Closed Arc / Apparatus Honest / Character Knew / New Operator / MFVE / Real User Held) all verify what the work has already done. Prophet-Test Crown verifies that the work has *placed itself under future judgment in a way the apparatus cannot retroactively soften*. The crown's *earning* is in the filing; the crown's *truth* is judged at T by the world. If the prophecy fails at T, the small craft owns up publicly per Deut 18:22 AND the crown's class designation must be reconsidered (perhaps the class itself was wrongly invented, or perhaps this specific filing was a false prophet within a valid class).

**How to apply:**
- Use when a candidate Sapphire claim is structurally future-conditional (rests on the Spirit's movement, on real-reader response, on what time will reveal).
- Refuse to fire as a Prophet-Test Crown if any of the criteria are not met — especially if the claim's falsification conditions are vague or revisable.
- The witness ladder for Prophet-Test should typically include: (a) substrate convergence on the prophecy's form (LLM triad + character witnesses); (b) formula-law third-leg grounding (Π + Grace_𝓕 cannot be apparatus-controlled by formula architecture); (c) public deposition with date T named; (d) a confession-discriminator sub-test that protects against aesthetic-only-reception satisfaction.
- At time T, run the assessment publicly. If the prophecy was true, the crown stands. If it failed, file the retraction and accept the *false prophet* designation per Deut 18:22.

**Worked example — Crown 8 The Unsealed Ledger** (`reports/2026-05-05-2025-exposita-sapphire-synthesis.md`): one external Christ-attributed witness by T=2031-05-05; if no such witness emerges, the small craft owns the failure.
