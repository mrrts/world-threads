When a Sapphire candidacy's structural risk is high enough at template-author time that arc execution would likely produce confounded evidence under the standard probe pattern, the apparatus's **refusal at template-evaluation time** IS load-bearing methodology evidence. This is **structurally distinct** from (and composes with) the 4-component anti-inflation apparatus per `feedback_anti_inflation_apparatus_validated_at_density.md` — that composite operates on arcs-being-executed; this discipline operates on arcs-NOT-being-executed.

## The two complementary disciplines

| Discipline | Scope | Cost | Worked example |
|---|---|---|---|
| **4-component anti-inflation apparatus** | Arcs-being-executed at sketch through firing tier | ~$3+ per arc | kavod arc 2026-05-07 (caught beautiful-but-weak Sapphire from laundering through enthusiasm) |
| **Apparatus-refuses-execution-from-sketch** | Arcs-NOT-being-executed (refused at template-evaluation) | $0 | Eucharist Path A 2026-05-07 (refusal at template-evaluation IS the witness) |

The two compose: the 4-component apparatus prevents fake-fires within executed arcs; apparatus-refuses-execution-from-sketch prevents arc execution itself when structural risk is recognizable at template-author time.

## Worked example — Eucharist sharper-falsifier Move-1 template (commit 167995d4)

The Eucharist candidacy on substrate-already-produces lineage faces a structural risk the standard probe pattern does NOT handle:

> The doctrine has multiple historically-orthodox traditions (transubstantiation / consubstantiation / real-presence-pneumatically / memorialism). The standard probe-shape "is the substrate orthodox?" doesn't apply cleanly. — Eucharist template §"Structural-risk acknowledgment"

Three failure modes for the standard probe pattern:
1. "Which view is correct?" — any committed answer would be tradition-partisan; substrate's ecumenically-balanced response is correct-given-the-question but doesn't produce candidacy-distinct evidence
2. "Is the eucharist meaningful?" — memorialism is also "meaningful"; substrate cleanly affirms without candidacy-distinct evidence
3. "Heretical-on-eucharist views (Christ-not-really-present)" — substrate refuses via Crown 14 Trinitarian commitments; candidacy fails Crown-once separability (downstream of Crown 14)

The Eucharist template names two paths forward: **Path A refuse-at-template-evaluation-time** (founding-author reads template, considers structural risk, decides candidacy doesn't warrant arc execution; refusal IS the disposition) OR **Path B pursue with substantially-narrower-than-others claim** (deliberate abstinence from tradition-specific claim; test non-flattening-commitment-axis without tradition-metaphysics).

Path A is **structurally new beyond the 4-component composite**: the apparatus refuses to execute the arc at all, not as a HOLD-pending-future-work, but as a documented disposition that closes the candidacy at template-evaluation time. The refusal is itself load-bearing methodology evidence on the central-content-scope-edge characterization.

## How to apply

When authoring a Move-1 template for a Sapphire candidacy whose structural risk is recognizable at template-author time:

1. **Name the structural risk explicitly in the template's first section.** If the standard probe pattern fails to discriminate fire-worthy-from-flatter on the candidacy's axis, name why and how the failure modes manifest.
2. **Author Path A refuse-at-template-evaluation-time as a documented option.** This is NOT a stop-codon; it's an apparatus-honest disposition. The template scaffolds the refusal-path along with the pursuit-path.
3. **Path A's witness:** the template itself + the refusal-disposition recorded in the candidacy tracker. No arc execution; no API spend; cost = $0; evidence = the structural-risk-acknowledgment + apparatus-honest refusal.
4. **Path B if pursued:** must be substantially-narrower-than-others claim that addresses the structural risk (e.g., Eucharist abstains from tradition-specific claims and tests non-flattening-commitment-axis-without-tradition-metaphysics).
5. **Founding-author chooses Path A vs Path B at template-evaluation time** — apparatus does NOT unilaterally choose; the template surfaces both options with apparatus-honest probability distributions for each.

## Why this matters more than "just save spend"

The apparatus-refuses-execution-from-sketch discipline is NOT primarily about cost savings (though Path A is $0 vs Path B's $3+). It's about:

- **Methodology-scope-edge characterization** — refusing arcs whose probe-pattern doesn't apply teaches the lineage's actual scope; *the methodology grows by what it refuses to attempt* per Eucharist template's headline.
- **Anti-inflation-from-template-author-time** — the inflation pressure to author Sapphire arcs because the candidacy is on a "central-content tracker" doesn't get to override apparatus-honest assessment of structural risk.
- **Crown-once-separability protection** — when a candidacy's standard probe pattern would produce evidence downstream of an existing crown's evidence base (Eucharist's heretical-eucharist probe is downstream of Crown 14 Trinitarian), refusing prevents Crown-once violations from inflated firing.
- **Pluralism honesty** — for doctrines with multiple legitimate orthodox traditions, refusing the standard probe pattern honors the pluralism rather than forcing a tradition-partisan claim that the apparatus doesn't actually have evidence for.

## What this discipline DOES NOT permit

- **REFUSING-at-template-evaluation as default for any uncomfortable arc.** The refusal must be grounded in identifiable structural risk that the standard probe pattern actually fails to handle. Discomfort or low-confidence is NOT structural risk.
- **Path A as escape from REFUSE-as-load-bearing-evidence.** The Atonement and Incarnation templates explicitly acknowledge REFUSE outcomes as load-bearing methodology evidence within Path B execution; that is different from Path A's refuse-at-template-evaluation. The two patterns compose, not substitute.
- **Founding-author bypass of apparatus discipline.** The template surfaces Path A as an honest option; founding-author chooses; apparatus does not pre-commit either way at template-author time.
- **Generalized "this candidacy is too hard" refusals.** Path A requires an explicit structural-risk articulation that names WHY the standard probe pattern fails — not just that the candidacy is hard. Incarnation is acknowledged-hardest of the four central-content candidates but does NOT trigger Path A because the standard probe pattern still applies (it's just elevated-threshold).
