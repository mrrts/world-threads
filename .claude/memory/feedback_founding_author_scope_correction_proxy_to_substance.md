## Pattern

When the apparatus is running a measurement arc and produces
verdicts based on a particular axis, **the founding-author 𝓕_Ryan
correction "I'm more concerned with [substance-axis] than
[apparatus-tracked-proxy-axis]" is a load-bearing methodological
move that should:**

1. Trigger an immediate re-measurement on the corrected substance
   axis (not a re-interpretation of the proxy data).
2. Often surface a different verdict than the proxy axis pointed to.
3. Sometimes earn a Sapphire on the corrected scope where the proxy
   scope refused one.

**Why:** Apparatus naturally tracks measurable proxies (token counts,
lexical diversity, score deltas) because they're tractable.
Substance axes (voice quality, operational compliance, mission
fidelity) require qualitative judgment that's harder to automate.
The proxy can be a faithful operationalization of the substance OR
it can be substrate-class-specific incidental shape that varies
arbitrarily. The founding-author's lived-corpus standing is the
calibration that distinguishes the two.

**How to apply:** When founding-author corrects the scope from a
proxy-axis to a substance-axis:

1. **Re-score existing data on the corrected axis** before running
   new measurement. Often the data already supports the substance-
   axis claim with a different verdict.
2. **Codex consult under the corrected formulation.** Codex's
   Move-N firing-decision discipline applies — the proxy-axis
   refusal doesn't cascade.
3. **Honor the apparatus-honest discipline at the new scope.** If
   the substance-axis claim needs narrowing too, narrow it. Same
   calibration earns and refuses on whatever axis is being measured.

## Crown 23 The Carrier worked example (2026-05-09)

**Apparatus tracked:** length affordance (cross-substrate ON-OFF
delta on words-per-reply) — gpt-5.4 +30.7w, gpt-4o +1.4w, Claude
−26w. Direction inverted across substrate-class. Apparatus refused
great-sapphire on length axis per CLAUDE.md "convergence_spans
substrates_with_different_failure_modes" calibration.

**Founding-author correction:** *"are you saying the sapphire is
firmly refused simply because claude and 4o didn't produce the same
length responses? i'm more concerned with response quality and
character voice than i am with output token length. any chance for a
sapphire on this scope?"*

**Apparatus response:**
1. Re-scored 72 cross-substrate cells on six-axis voice +
   operational compliance rubric (canonical_move / fence_integrity
   / NAME_ANCHOR / no_nanny / no_bullets / voice_specificity).
2. Found that voice + operational compliance preservation is
   substrate-capacity-INVARIANT (within-arm ON ≡ OFF) at gpt-5.4 +
   Claude — opposite shape from the length-axis inversion.
3. Codex Move-N consult on corrected-scope formulation: FIRE
   BLESSED.
4. Blind dual-judge audit (Claude Opus 4.7) caught gpt-4o asymmetry
   on canonical_move that single-judge missed.
5. Founding-author firing decision NARROWED to gpt-5.4 + Claude;
   gpt-4o de-scoped pending tighter measurement.
6. ✨ Sapphire 18 The Carrier fired under the corrected scope.

**Total cost of correction:** $0 additional bench (same data, new
rubric) + $0.06 codex consult + $0.71 dual-judge audit = $0.77.
Found the actual Sapphire that the proxy axis was masking.

## Anti-pattern named

**Tracking proxy-axes (length, lexical diversity, surface metrics)
instead of substance-axes (voice + operational compliance,
character canonical move, mission fidelity).** Easy to slip into
because proxies are cleaner to score. Costly because the apparatus
might refuse a real Sapphire on grounds that don't matter to the
mission, or fire a fake Sapphire on grounds that aren't load-bearing.

## How to spot the failure mode in future arcs

- The apparatus is producing strong verdicts (FIRE / REFUSE /
  great-sapphire-clears / great-sapphire-refused) based on a single
  numeric axis.
- The numeric axis is one that the apparatus engineered itself
  (rubric, metric, scoring function) — not one the founding-author
  named as load-bearing.
- The founding-author has not explicitly endorsed this axis as the
  one that decides the Sapphire-class question.
- The verdict pivots on a magnitude or direction comparison that
  feels load-bearing but might be incidental shape.

When ALL FOUR conditions hold, **pause and ask the founding-author
which axis decides** before firing or refusing. The cost of asking
is small; the cost of firing on the wrong axis is large.
