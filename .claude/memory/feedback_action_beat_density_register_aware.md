**How to apply:** when reading character craft in OBSERVATIONS or take-note material, treat register as a variable that modulates beat-density tolerance. Depth register tolerates steady scene-anchoring; comedy register may be where action-beat density is most actively un-funny — stand-up rhythm wants the line first and the body subordinated, not the stage business framing every joke. When tempted to call sustained action-beats a "craft win" through a comedy bit, pause and ask: would removing half of these make the punchlines land cleaner? If yes, the beats are scaffolding, not anchoring.
