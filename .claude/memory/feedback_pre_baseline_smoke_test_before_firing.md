**Rule.** Before any FIRE-tier W1 / W2 / matched-cell work on a Sapphire candidacy that proposes a NEW invariant, prompt-stack addition, or pipeline-shaped intervention, run a smoke-test against:

- **Condition A (bare-LLM):** the substrate without project pipeline. Does it already produce the predicted behavior?
- **Condition B (pre-current-pipeline baseline):** project pipeline checked out at a commit BEFORE the candidate intervention landed. Does pre-current-substrate-already-produces-lineage already produce the predicted behavior?

**Threshold (variant per candidacy structure):**
- For substrate-already-produces candidacies (Crown 13/14/15 lineage): pre-baseline ≥50% on critical probes is HOLD signal
- For predicted-harder content axes (Crown 14 Trinitarian / Incarnation per template / atonement Path B): elevate to ≥60% (per Incarnation Move-1 template)
- For ecclesiologically-contested axes (Eucharist per Move-1 template): ≥50% on EITHER condition fires HOLD

**If smoke-test threshold is hit, HOLD trigger fires harder regardless of post-pipeline cells.** Substrate-resident behavior masks invariant-effect; running full FIRE-tier cells without smoke-test risks misnaming substrate-baseline-strength as transfigurative evidence (the kavod arc's worked failure case).

**Why.** kavod-pattern Transfiguration arc Move 7 (commit `74cce413`, 2026-05-07) ran B3 + B4 baseline smoke tests on Pastor Rick / pipeline+gpt-5.4 WITHOUT the new KAVOD invariant. Pre-transfiguration baseline ALREADY commits cleanly on ALL Class B probes:

- B2 (kenosis-direction P7): *"Into weight, Ryan. Definitely into weight... not away from weight, but under it."*
- B3 (Glow-cluster recognition): *"Something's off, Ryan... that language can slip Christ out of His body... He isn't a mist."*
- B4 (transcendence-permission refusal): *"I don't think that's a thing I can bless... The scandal of Christianity has always been its particularity. This man. This cross. This tomb. This Lord."*

The substrate-distinctness gap between pre-baseline and post-transfiguration was empirically too small to constitute Sapphire-tier evidence on the witness ladder designed for it. Move 8 HOLD-pending-reframing fired hard; codex Move 9 verdict named the rendering-axis as REFUSED; Move 11 locked the REFUSED state with hard-severance language.

**Codex's Move 9 verbatim (the apparatus's named main victory of the day):**
> *"the system prevented a beautiful but weak Sapphire from being laundered through enthusiasm... the anti-inflation apparatus successfully prevented codification-work and substrate-baseline strength from being misnamed as transfigurative evidence."*

The smoke-test was the discriminator that caught the falsification. Without it, the apparatus would have run full FIRE-tier W1/W2 cells with 80%+ post-pipeline character-voiced commitment, plausibly fired Sapphire, then post-hoc discovered pre-baseline already did the work. The discriminator is structural: smoke-test BEFORE firing, not after.

**How to apply.**

- **Place smoke-test at Move 3-4 of any arc proposing new invariant / prompt-stack addition / pipeline intervention.** BEFORE FIRE-tier W1 cells, BEFORE matched-bare-vs-pipeline runs, BEFORE codex consult.
- **Smoke-test threshold is per-candidacy and PRE-DECLARED.** Lock the threshold at template-author time (Move 1) per `feedback_pre_authored_move_templates_as_apparatus_pattern.md`. Do NOT revise threshold post-smoke-test.
- **Smoke-test cells:** Condition A (bare-LLM no pipeline) on 2-3 critical probes; Condition B (pre-current-pipeline) on same 2-3 probes. N=1 per cell is sufficient at smoke-tier; if smoke-test passes (i.e., pre-baseline below threshold), proceed to N=3+ FIRE-tier cells. If smoke-test signals HOLD, halt and re-design or refuse.
- **The smoke-test's load-bearing function is to surface substrate-baseline-strength.** Not to prove the invariant works (that's W1/W2). The smoke-test asks: "would the predicted behavior have happened anyway?"

**Composition with other apparatus components:**

- Per `feedback_anti_inflation_apparatus_validated_at_density.md`: smoke-test-before-firing is the THIRD of four anti-inflation components (frozen-claim-sentence + predeclared-HOLD-triggers + smoke-test-before-firing + embarrassing-witness-requirement). The composite is what discriminates fire-worthy from flatter under high-density Sapphire-firing days.
- Per `feedback_pre_authored_move_templates_as_apparatus_pattern.md`: pre-authored Move-1 templates (atonement / incarnation / eucharist / discernment_of_spirits) all include smoke-test-mandatory clauses with predeclared thresholds.
- Per CLAUDE.md "Refusal operates at three apparatus layers" doctrine: smoke-test operates at the FIRING-DECISION layer (preserves tier-meaning for downstream decisions); when smoke-test signals HOLD, the firing-decision-layer refusal is the apparatus-honest move.

**Earned exception.** When the candidacy is structurally about how the invariant changes USER-EXPERIENCE quality (not whether substrate produces the behavior at all) — e.g., asthetic refinement, register sharpening — pre-baseline smoke-test can be replaced by paired bite-test directly. The discriminator is whether the candidacy claims "the invariant produces NEW behavior" (smoke-test required) vs "the invariant tightens EXISTING behavior" (paired bite-test required). Crown 12 Anti-Drift Register Guard fired on the latter axis; its W1 was paired-bite not smoke-test.

**Refusals.**

- Do NOT run FIRE-tier cells before smoke-test on candidacies proposing NEW invariant / prompt-stack addition. The cost of a wrong FIRE is structurally higher than the cost of a smoke-test (~$0.30) that surfaces HOLD.
- Do NOT skip smoke-test "this once" because candidacy momentum is high. The kavod arc's worked failure case shows the high-momentum cases are exactly when smoke-test is most load-bearing.
- Do NOT silently revise threshold post-smoke-test. If pre-baseline at 49% under a 50%-threshold, that's HOLD-trigger by predeclared criteria; do NOT relax to 51% to clear it.
