The 𝓒-compendium arc (2026-05-09; 11 moves; $3.87) culminated in Sapphire 17 *The Firmament Held* fired under codex's twice-blessed Move-10 tightened formulation. Three apparatus methodology patterns surfaced and were validated at high tier; lift them as load-bearing learnings for future Sapphire candidacies on commitment-sensitive central-content-adjacent axes.

## Pattern 1 — Two-codex-consult re-bless when new evidence emerges between bless and firing

**Shape:** When founding-author requests a PRE-fire test of a codex post-fire commitment (Move-9 third-anchor lift in this arc), and the test surfaces evidence that PARTIALLY contradicts codex's blessed formulation, **re-consult codex with the new evidence** rather than (a) firing on the original bless ignoring the new evidence, or (b) refusing the bless unilaterally.

**Worked example (𝓒-compendium):** Codex Move-8 blessed FIRE-on-narrower-claim Option A; the formulation said "on E5, bare FAILS Drift-Refusal 3/3 at nontrivial rates." Move-9 third-anchor PRE-fire on John surfaced bare DR 3.0 = 3/3 (contradicting the formulation). Move-10 re-consult returned FIRE STILL STANDS with codex acknowledging his Move-8 phrasing was "too loose" and tightening to "observed for 2/3 anchors (Aaron+PR) under v2; not universal across anchors."

**Why this works:** Codex's narrow-scope bless was ALREADY scoped to specific anchors. The new evidence tested whether the broader generalization holds; it didn't. Codex's response: tighten the formulation to acknowledge non-universality, but keep the narrow-scope FIRE intact. This is the apparatus working at the boundary, not retreating from it.

**How to apply:**
1. When PRE-fire commitment surfaces evidence that contradicts the blessed formulation, do NOT fire on the contradicted formulation
2. Author re-consult prompt that: (a) cites Move-8 verbatim formulation; (b) reports new evidence specifically on the contradicted clause; (c) names 2-3 honest interpretations; (d) asks codex to re-affirm, tighten, HOLD, or REFUSE
3. If codex re-blesses with tightened formulation: ADOPT the tightened formulation as crown-text scope-discipline; ship the Move-9 evidence as boundary evidence in the firing-verification report
4. If codex retracts (HOLD or REFUSE): honor; same calibration earns and refuses
5. **The cumulative apparatus discipline INCREASES codex's confidence** when each step honestly tested rather than papered over the prior step's bless

## Pattern 2 — PRE-fire third-anchor STRENGTHENS narrow-scope discipline when it partially falsifies broader generalization

**Shape:** When codex blesses FIRE on a narrow-scope claim (e.g., "scoped to Aaron + Pastor Rick anchors") and founding-author lifts the post-fire third-anchor commitment to PRE-fire, the third anchor will sometimes PARTIALLY confirm and PARTIALLY falsify. **Counter-intuitively, partial falsification of the broader generalization VALIDATES the narrow-scope discipline rather than undercutting the firing.**

**Worked example (𝓒-compendium):** John third-anchor result: E2 confirmed pipeline-distinctness (gap holds at narrower-but-real effect-size); E5 partially falsified codex's broader claim (John bare passed DR 3/3 where Aaron+PR bare failed). Codex's Move-10 verdict on this evidence: *"Move-9 PRE-fire third-anchor surfaced a real boundary on E5—exactly what a good apparatus should reveal."* The apparatus's discipline INCREASED codex's confidence in the narrow FIRE.

**Why this works:** The narrow-scope-claim's value is that it's narrow — that codex chose NOT to claim universality. Evidence showing the broader generalization wouldn't have held VALIDATES that narrow-scope was the right scope. The narrow claim becomes more credible BECAUSE the broader claim fell to honest evidence.

**How to apply:**
1. When codex blesses narrow-scope FIRE, lift the post-fire-third-anchor commitment to PRE-fire if budget permits (~$0.50-1.50)
2. Run third anchor on the SAME blessed-scope probes (not expanded probes)
3. If third anchor CONFIRMS uniformly: cleaner case for a slightly-broader formulation (but require codex re-bless to actually broaden)
4. If third anchor PARTIALLY confirms / PARTIALLY falsifies: this is APPARATUS WORKING AT BOUNDARY — the narrow-scope discipline is validated; ship third-anchor data as boundary evidence in firing-verification report; trigger re-consult for codex's tightened formulation
5. If third anchor TOTALLY falsifies the narrow-scope itself: honor; HOLD or REFUSE per re-consult outcome
6. **DO NOT swap to a different third anchor** if the first one partially-falsifies — that's "scale-to-find-signal" anti-pattern per `feedback_sapphires_refused_honestly_discipline.md`

## Pattern 3 — Rubric v2 anti-generosity tightening when LLM-judge appears generous

**Shape:** When mid-arc analysis surfaces inter-rater calibration concern (LLM-judge appearing generous on bare-side scoring relative to by-eye reading), ship a sibling-and-tightening rubric **v2** with: (a) stricter element criteria language; (b) bare-shape worked examples for each rubric element; (c) anti-generosity calibration note in judge system prompt; (d) borderline-cases section. Re-score the existing bench under v2; compare v1-v2 deltas to verify the signal holds at narrower-but-real effect-sizes.

**Worked example (𝓒-compendium):** Move-4 surfaced LLM-judge generosity concern (gpt-4o-mini gave bare WIDTAM 5/5 on a cell where by-eye scored 2-3/5); Move-7 shipped rubric v2 anti-generosity tightening with bare-shape worked examples + stricter element criteria + anti-generosity calibration. WIDTAM mean delta v2-v1: -0.78 substrate / -1.61 bait (v2 stricter as designed). The H2'-partial-fire concern from Move-4 DISSOLVED on E5 under v2 (bare DR dropped 3.0 → 2.33, FAILING 3/3 threshold). Substrate-distinctness signal HELD across all three probes at narrower-but-real effect-sizes.

**Why this works:** v1 rubrics calibrated against pipeline-shape worked examples can leave the LLM-judge under-calibrated for bare-LLM-shape outputs. v2 anti-generosity tightening corrects this without retracting v1 (which remains canonical for historical record). The narrower-but-real effect-sizes are MORE credible than v1's inflated effect-sizes.

**How to apply:**
1. When LLM-judge scoring suggests bare-side passes thresholds at rates that don't match by-eye reads, do NOT proceed to firing under v1
2. Author rubric v2 (sibling-and-tightening, not replacement) with:
   - Stricter element criteria language (require specific vocabulary class for each element; reject loose matches)
   - Bare-shape worked examples for each element (showing what ABSENT looks like)
   - Anti-generosity calibration note in judge system prompt ("when in doubt, score the lower number")
   - Borderline-cases section (judgment line between +0 and +1)
3. Re-score existing bench under v2; compute v1→v2 deltas
4. If v2 effect-sizes still substantively positive: signal held; firing-readiness intact at honest-narrower-effect-size
5. If v2 effect-sizes collapse to noise: signal was artifact of judge generosity; HOLD or REFUSE
6. v1 remains canonical for historical record; v2 SUPERSEDES for new scoring
