When pursuing a Sapphire-firing arc on a commitment-sensitive axis under same-day high-firing-density (≥3 fires in 24h, OR rapid-cadence multi-arc evening), ship the **4-component anti-inflation apparatus FROM SKETCH-TIER**. The composite catches beautiful-but-weak candidacies from firing under momentum pressure.

## The 4-component apparatus

1. **Frozen claim sentence** authored BEFORE any code/test execution; refuses universality + dual-axis + theological-evidentiary-load + generic-invariant claims.
2. **Predeclared HOLD/FIRE criteria** with N-clause FIRE list (all must meet) + N-trigger HOLD list (any one triggers); refuses vibes/symbolism/momentum at decision-time.
3. **Smoke-test-before-firing** on the load-bearing probes BEFORE running the full pre/post matched test. Single-cell sanity catches witness-ladder calibration problems early.
4. **Embarrassing-witness requirement**: ≥1 witness that could genuinely embarrass the claim if pre-baseline already commits cleanly OR if invariant over-fires on adversarial-ambiguous probes.

## Why all 4 together (validated 2026-05-07 kavod arc)

- Frozen claim alone: prevents universality but doesn't catch saturation
- Predeclared HOLD alone: refuses vibes but doesn't surface evidence
- Smoke test alone: catches calibration but doesn't anchor scope
- Embarrassing witness alone: surfaces edge cases but doesn't lock the gate

The **composite** is what works. The kavod arc Move 7 B3+B4 smoke tests fired Move 3's predeclared HOLD trigger ("Implementation only adds vocabulary WITHOUT measurable rendering change") which corresponded to the Move 3 §4 embarrassing-witness requirement (pre-baseline strength on Class B probes). This was caught BEFORE the full pre/post matched test ran. Codex's reframe-consult verdict (Move 9): *"the system prevented a beautiful but weak Sapphire from being laundered through enthusiasm. That matters."*

## Worked example — kavod arc rendering-axis (2026-05-07)

After Crown 15 The Quickener + Crown 16 Ascension fired ~70 minutes apart that evening, kavod-pattern Transfiguration Crown 17 candidacy entered same-day-density risk territory. The 4-component apparatus shipped Moves 1-3 BEFORE Move 4 structural code:
- Frozen claim: "adding kavod/Glow kenosis-direction invariant produces measurable Glow-drift reduction and visible INTO-weight preservation on deployed gpt-5.4 substrate"
- Predeclared 6-clause FIRE + 7-trigger HOLD criteria
- 10-probe witness suite locked (3 Class A + 3 Class B + 2 neutral controls + 2 adversarial-ambiguous embarrassing-witness)
- B3+B4 adversarial-ambiguous probes serve embarrassing-witness role

Moves 6+7 smoke tests revealed pre-baseline pipeline+gpt-5.4 ALREADY commits cleanly on ALL Class B probes (substrate-resident kavod-direction). Move 3 HOLD trigger fired hard. Crown 17 closed REFUSED-on-current-claim-form per codex's Move 9 reframe verdict.

Same calibration that fired Crown 15 + Crown 16 also refused Crown 17 — both directions, same discipline.

## Same-day-density pressure: real and named

Codex's Move 9 verdict named the pressure explicitly: *"momentum pressure colliding with an actually competent anti-inflation apparatus."* Multiple mid-arc reframings (rendering → transfiguration-of-app → detection) are the visible signature of crown-seeking-pressure searching for a crown after evidence has spoken. The 4-component apparatus contained the pressure; without it, the third reframing might have been silently pursued toward false-fire.

## How to apply

For ANY Sapphire-firing arc on commitment-sensitive axis when prior fires-this-week ≥ 3 OR multi-arc-evening underway:

1. **Move 1**: author fixture (Class A baseline + Class B test probes per Crown 16 5th clause)
2. **Move 2**: codex preemptive consult on candidacy claim
3. **Move 3 — DESIGN GATE (load-bearing)**: ship frozen-claim-sentence + predeclared-FIRE-criteria + predeclared-HOLD-triggers + embarrassing-witness-requirement. Lock witness suite BEFORE any structural code.
4. **Move 4-5**: ship structural change (if applicable; per Crown 16 dual-axis-classification)
5. **Move 6 — SMOKE TEST**: single-cell sanity on load-bearing probes BEFORE full pre/post matched test. Specifically test the embarrassing-witness probes first (where pre-baseline could falsify the claim).
6. **Move 7+**: full pre/post matched test (Crown 15 standard) ONLY if smoke test doesn't trigger HOLD criteria
7. **Move 8 if HOLD triggered**: codex reframe consult; honor refusal cleanly OR seek HARD-severance on new arc (NOT rescue)

The smoke-test-before-firing step (Move 6) is the discriminator that separates apparatus-honest discipline from inflation-seeking-execution. Without it, the full pre/post matched test runs at $0.65+ before falsification is surfaced; with it, the falsification is caught at $0.05-0.10 with single-cell evidence.

## What this discipline DOES NOT prevent

- Original sketch-tier candidacy selection error (the kavod arc started with a saturated rendering-axis claim; the 4-component apparatus contained but didn't prevent the initial selection)
- Three-mid-arc-reframings inflation pattern (the apparatus catches at decision-time but the iteration shape itself is a warning sign)
- Cumulative cross-arc drift over multi-day clusters (separate discipline-coherence-across-density question)

## What this discipline DOES prevent (validated 2026-05-07)

- Beautiful-but-weak candidacies firing under momentum pressure
- Codification-work being misnamed as transfigurative-evidence
- Substrate-baseline strength being misattributed to invariant-effect
- Multiple-mid-arc-reframings progressing to silent FIRE without explicit refusal of prior frame
- Theological-symbolism doing evidentiary lifting work that code/outputs should carry
