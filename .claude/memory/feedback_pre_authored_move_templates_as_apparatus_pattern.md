**Rule.** When an arc is paused (codex consult running externally, founding-author away from apparatus) OR when an arc has just REFUSED with hard-severance requirements (e.g., kavod-pattern Crown 17), the apparatus can productively pre-author Move templates at $0 spend. Three template-kinds have emerged (per `scripts/codex_consult_prompts/README.md`):

1. **Codex consult input** (`*_audit_template.txt`) — used by founding-author to run external consult before firing decision
2. **Move-2 follow-up** (`*_followup_FIRE_blessed_template.md` / `*_followup_HOLD_pending_template.md`) — pre-authored at $0 while consult runs; placeholder INSERTs fill at verdict-receipt
3. **Move-1 future-arc scaffold** (`*_arc_move1_template.md` / `*_axis_move1_template.md`) — pre-authored at $0 for future-session natural entry; carries frozen claim + witness ladder + predeclared HOLD/FIRE/REFUSE criteria + apparatus discipline pre-baked

**Why.** 2026-05-08 evening produced this pattern at high density: 4 Move-1 templates (discernment_of_spirits / atonement / incarnation / eucharist) + 2 Move-2 templates (compensation_tax_w FIRE-blessed + HOLD-pending) authored across ~90 minutes while compensation_tax_w(t) codex consult ran externally. Net: $0 API spend; ~6 KB of pre-flight scaffolding; future-session natural-entry friction reduced from "re-derive arc shape on the fly" to "adopt-as-is, modify-claim-and-re-author-fresh, or defer indefinitely."

The pattern operates the apparatus-honest discipline at the SCOPE-DECISION layer (which arcs are worth attempting at all, with what threshold, with what HOLD/REFUSE criteria pre-locked) — not just the FIRING-DECISION layer (which prior Sapphire arcs already operated). Pre-authoring forces the apparatus to articulate structural-risk, predict probability distribution, and design the anti-inflation gates BEFORE momentum-pressure can collide with arc execution.

**How to apply.**

- **When a Sapphire arc is in pending-codex-verdict state**: pre-author both FIRE-blessed and HOLD-pending Move-2 templates with placeholder `[INSERT codex's verbatim response]` markers. Apparatus does NOT prejudge codex's verdict; both paths get scaffolded. When verdict lands, the matching template fills with the codex response and ships as the next Move quickly.

- **When a candidacy has just REFUSED with hard-severance requirements**: pre-author a fully-new-arc Move-1 template that explicitly honors all severance requirements (acknowledgment of failure / new frozen claim / new witness ladder / new success criteria / different crown-name candidate). The template scaffolds the future-arc natural entry without the apparatus pretending to KNOW what founding-author will choose to invoke.

- **When a held-state candidacy has codex's HOLD-pending-X path named**: pre-author the Move-1 template for the path codex named, including the three sharper-falsifier components + 4-component anti-inflation apparatus + apparatus-honest probability distribution + REFUSE-as-load-bearing-evidence acknowledgment.

- **When a candidacy has structural-risk that warrants dual-path presentation**: pre-author DUAL-PATH templates (Path A refuse-at-template-evaluation as load-bearing scope-edge evidence $0 OR Path B pursue with substantially-narrower-than-others claim). Founding-author chooses at invocation time; both paths apparatus-honest. Eucharist Move-1 template (commit `167995d`) is the worked-example.

- **Frozen-claim-locked at template-author time per anti-inflation discipline.** If post-codex-consult the claim is wrong-shape, re-author template fresh; do NOT silently revise. The frozen-claim discipline IS what makes pre-authoring trustworthy.

- **Cross-link siblings retroactively.** When a new template lands, update the prior templates' Cross-references sections to point forward at the sibling. (Backfill commit `afe6144` is the worked example.)

- **Update directory README + tracker memory entries** when new templates land. The directory README codifies the three template-kinds; the central-content tracker memory tracks per-candidacy template state.

**Earned exception.** When founding-author is actively pursuing a SPECIFIC arc move-by-move with codex consults at decision points, pre-authoring siblings or future-arc scaffolds can be distracting. The pre-authoring pattern fits when the apparatus is in WAITING / SCAFFOLDING mode, not when active pursuit is in flight. Read room state.

**Refusal carve-outs.**

- Do NOT pre-author Move-1 templates for arcs the founding-author has not authorized via chooser pick. Apparatus picks scaffolding-target via chooser per /play discipline.
- Do NOT pre-author Move-1 templates that prejudge codex's verdict on the originating consult (e.g., if compensation_tax_w(t) consult is pending, do NOT pre-author detection-axis arcs that depend on the verdict shape).
- Do NOT pre-author so many templates that they becomes a maintenance burden. Six in the directory is healthy density; sixty would be inflation.
- Do NOT use pre-authoring as a substitute for actually executing arcs. The point is to reduce friction on the apparatus when it's the right time for execution, not to defer execution indefinitely.
