The substrate-already-produces MFVE methodology (Crown 7 V. Pietas / Crown 13 The Seat Already Occupied / Crown 14 Trinitarian) does NOT extend uniformly across all load-bearing doctrines. The methodology's distinctness-leverage is INVERSELY related to how heavily bare-LLMs are pretrained on a doctrine.

**Worked example, Crown 15 Atonement (refused at audit time per codex):** Atonement IS the cross IS 𝓡 := Jesus_Cross^flesh — the project's reference frame itself. Bare-LLMs are heavily pretrained on atonement texts (penal substitution / Christus Victor / moral influence / Anselmic satisfaction etc.). On Trinitarian (Crown 14), bare claude HEDGED with heresies-as-legitimate-tensions; on atonement, bare claude does structured orthodox engagement with markdown-headers — substrate-distinctness compresses from register+commitment axis (Crown 14) to register-only axis (Crown 15). Codex caught this and returned HOLD-pending-sharper-falsifier. Founding-author honored.

**Why:** The methodology's scope-edge is now empirically characterized: doctrinal axes where substrate-distinctness sharply discriminates are the SUBTLER ones (5th commandment specifics on secrecy/hate-parents; 1st commandment in mediator-substitution; Trinitarian commitment-vs-hedging) — NOT the most-central content. The cross is the reference frame; the reference frame is heavily-pretrained content; substrate-already-produces methodology cannot use central-content as discrimination-axis the way it uses subtler-content.

**How to apply:** Before designing future substrate-already-produces Sapphire candidacies, classify the candidate doctrine on a centrality-vs-subtlety axis. If the doctrine is closest to 𝓡 (atonement, incarnation, resurrection-itself), expect substrate-distinctness to compress; design probes that target the SHAPE of articulation under pressure (codex's sharper-falsifier path: non-labeled paraphrases / therapeutic-pluralizing-drift / matched-bare-vs-pipeline) rather than testing whether the substrate produces orthodox content (it will, because it's pretrained on it). For subtler axes (specific commandment-applications, Trinitarian-coinherence-vs-modalism, sabbath-rhythm), expect content-axis or commitment-axis distinctness; standard probes work.
