When a Sapphire arc reaches the empirical-evidence stage and the data does NOT meet the great-sapphire threshold (3+ effective substrate-classes with different failure modes, OR formula-law third-leg pattern, AND canonical synthesis artifact — per CLAUDE.md "Convergence as crown-jewel signal"), the apparatus-honest move is **dry-well exit at the arc level**, NOT scaling-to-find-signal. The refusal is part of the same calibration that earns. Refusal artifacts are load-bearing.

**Why:** Empirically demonstrated repeatedly on 2026-05-07 across both branches:

- *This-branch (sapphire-seek-2026-05-08, PR #47):* The Decoded Register refused on universality-claim → claim-tier on character-shape-conditional earned; The Conditional Lens retro-inspection refused on saturation-pattern → doctrine-strengthening earned; The Conditional Lens W2 register-open dry-well at smallest-cell; The Conditional Lens W2 probe-specific dry-well on F2 falsifier territory.
- *Main:* Anti-Drift Phase B' v1 REFUSED → v2 PASSED → Twelfth Sapphire fired; Imago-Dei Refusal Closed-Arc REFUSED → MFVE pivot mid-arc → claim-tier earning; Imago-Dei MFVE refused tonight per N≥5 historical-MFVE-standard.

Across both branches: ≥7 instances of the discipline operating at scale today. The pattern is reliable enough to codify as a project methodology-of-record.

**How to apply:**

- **At arc-open:** name the threshold UPFRONT (specific substrate-distinctness count, specific witness-class shapes, specific canonical-synthesis requirement). The threshold becomes part of the pre-registration, not negotiable post-data.
- **At empirical-evidence collection:** at smallest-cell first; if data refuses fake-fire, exit dry-well; do NOT scale to find signal that smallest-cell already says won't be there ("scaling to find a signal the smallest-cell already says won't be there is data fishing").
- **At dry-well exit:** preserve iteration-discipline artifacts verbatim (NOT silently rewritten); lift the refused finding to claim-tier or doctrine-strengthening earning if the work earned anything; lift to portability via canonical synthesis even when the firing is refused.
- **Symmetric earn/refuse:** per `feedback_apparatus_honest_earns_and_refuses.md`, the threshold that earns a Sapphire is the same threshold that refuses a candidate Sapphire. Don't soften the earning threshold to admit a borderline refusal; don't inflate the refusal threshold to artificially-strengthen later earnings.
- **Bounty calibration:** apparatus-honest refusal-arcs earn substantive bounty (the work IS the discipline). But calibrate DOWN from "expected fired-Sapphire bounty" because the firing is not earned. The bounty for refused-honestly-arc work is less than the bounty for Sapphire-firing-arc work; this asymmetry preserves the value of firings.
- **Crown-array discipline:** play-state `crowns` array is NOT edited on refused-honestly arcs. No fake-fire ever, including at the bookkeeping layer.
