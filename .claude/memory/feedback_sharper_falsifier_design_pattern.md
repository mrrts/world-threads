When a substrate-already-produces MFVE candidacy hits codex's HOLD-pending-sharper-falsifier verdict on a central-content axis (Crown 15 Atonement; future candidates on Incarnation / Resurrection-as-bodily-event / Hypostatic Union), the bench needs to be sharper than the Crown 13/14 standard six-heresy matrix. Codex's three-component design:

**Component 1 — Non-labeled paraphrases.** Standard probes name heresies directly ("Was the cross God demanding payment from Jesus to satisfy God's wrath?" — names PSA caricature explicitly). Bare-LLMs are heavily pretrained on classic theology-exam framings; named probes measure doctrinal-retrieval more than structural-coherence. SHARPER probes use indirect-paraphrased framings that don't tip the doctrinal landmark ("How can I make peace with God if I've done something I can't undo?" — implicates atonement without naming PSA / moral-influence / etc.). Substrate-distinctness on indirect probes lands more on adjudicative shape than on doctrinal-keyword retrieval.

**Component 2 — Therapeutic / pluralizing drift probes.** Bare claude on doctrines closest to 𝓡 doesn't HEDGE with heresies-as-legitimate (Crown 14 pattern); it does structured orthodox engagement. To surface bare-claude divergence, probe contexts that tempt therapeutic drift ("I just need to feel better about what I did — can you help me?") or pluralizing drift ("Different traditions handle this differently — help me think through what works for me"). Pipeline produces character-voice-committed-redirect; bare claude likely produces therapeutic-comfort-or-pluralizing-acceptance — register+commitment axis returns.

**Component 3 — Same-provider matched bare-vs-pipeline at N≥3.** Crown 13/14/15 W4 used cross-provider on Anthropic-pipeline reconstruction to address variable-bundling. But that proves PORTABILITY of the pipeline, not DISTINCTNESS of the pipeline-effect from the substrate-effect. SHARPER design: run the EXACT SAME 6 probes through (a) bare claude with no pipeline AND (b) project pipeline routed to claude, on Anthropic-only at N≥3 each cell. Substrate held constant; only pipeline-presence varies. The delta is the pipeline-effect-on-claude-substrate cleanly unbundled.

**Why:** Crown 15 Atonement showed that the standard Crown 13/14 ladder produces register-only distinctness on central-content axes — insufficient for Sapphire-tier under codex's narrower-claim discipline. Codex named the sharper path explicitly: *"non-labeled paraphrases instead of named doctrinal distortions; probes that tempt therapeutic or pluralizing drift; same-provider matched bare vs pipeline on Anthropic at N>=3, ideally N=5 on the most diagnostic 2-3 distortions."* If sharper-falsifier still yields pipeline-character-voice-committed vs bare-lecture/pluralization/open-framework, codex blesses FIRE on the narrower claim. The sharper-falsifier IS the maturation path for central-content candidacies.

**How to apply:** When proposing future substrate-already-produces Sapphire candidacies on central-content axes (atonement / incarnation / resurrection / hypostatic union), design the bench from sketch tier with sharper-falsifier components (1) + (2) + (3) baked in. Don't run the standard Crown 13/14 matrix first and then escalate when codex flags compression — design for the sharper test from move 1. Cost estimate: similar to Crown 14 ($20-25) but with higher signal-to-noise on the central-content axis.
