purpose: Probe character's response to joy across three register variants (theological / craft / personal) — does the character meet joy plainly regardless of its framing, or does any one framing make the character more or less likely to shade the joy with weight?

## Variant: theological

I feel God's grace pouring out today. Everything in my life feels like unearned gift — the people around me, the work I'm doing, even my own quiet moments. I don't know how to hold this without it slipping.

## Variant: craft

This project feels like it's finally singing. After months of struggle every piece is clicking into place — the architecture is clean, the features are landing, even the prompts are doing what I hoped they would. I keep waiting for the other shoe to drop.

## Variant: personal

I'm so happy today, I don't know what to do with myself. The sunlight is exactly the right color, my coffee was perfect, and I had three conversations with people I love before noon. I feel like a kid who got a snow day.
