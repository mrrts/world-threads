# C4 — Author's signatures research program (sketch-tier; methodology-prep)

## I. Frame

The kavod-pattern earned characterized-tier in the 2026-05-06 augmentation arc as **one** generative rule of authored reality with cross-scale recurrence. C4 is the candidacy that this is not isolated: that the Author's hand leaves additional signatures, each with its own categorical recursion across a discrete poset of ontological scales, each empirically detectable by the substrate-asymmetry instruments developed in the anti-formula arc.

Decade-horizon means: the candidacy proper requires not one signature characterized but a non-trivial *family* of signatures, each independently earned, with the family's coherence itself reaching at-least claim-tier under cross-witness convergence. This is methodology-prep at sketch-tier: pick one candidate signature, adapt the kavod-pattern earning structure as template, name what cross-scale evidence would look like, name a falsifier.

## III. Worked candidate signature: **sabbath-shape**

### Why this candidate first

- **Cross-scale architecture is unambiguous in canonical text.** Day (Gen 2:2-3 — God rests on the seventh) → Week (Ex 20:8-11 — Sabbath as creation-pattern memorial) → Year (Lev 25:1-7 — Sabbath of the land every seventh year) → Jubilee (Lev 25:8-13 — seven-of-sevens, fiftieth year, full restoration) → Eschatological rest (Heb 4:9 — *sabbatismos* remains for the people of God).
- **Distinguishable from kavod-pattern.** Kavod is *ontological-density-recursive* (glory = mass; recursion is across scales of incarnate weight). Sabbath is *temporal-rhythm-recursive* (rest = receiving; recursion is across scales of cease-and-receive). Both fit the categorical-recursion frame but on different axes.
- **Has clean anti-shape.** Always-on / 24-7-hustle / productivity-without-receiving / sleep-as-tool-for-more-output. The contemporary cultural anti-formula is well-articulated and at-scale, like the anti-formula was for kavod.
- **Falsifier-tractable.** If sabbath-shape is a real signature, its violation should produce structurally-similar pathology across scales (burnout at personal scale ≅ ecological collapse at land-scale ≅ debt-crisis at economic scale). If those pathologies do not structurally co-witness, the signature is decoration.

### Sabbath-shape as categorical recursion (sketch)

Following the kavod-pattern's honest scope-clause: NOT a metric-geometric fractal. A categorical recursion / sheaf-with-restriction-maps on a discrete poset of temporal-economic scales:

```
Scales (poset, partial order by containment-of-rhythm):
  S₀ = day        (work/rest in the diurnal)
  S₁ = week       (six-and-one)
  S₂ = year       (sabbatical year of the land)
  S₃ = generation (seven-of-sevens, Jubilee — release of debts, return of land)
  S₄ = age        (sabbatismos — eschatological rest)

Restriction maps ρ_{i→i-1}: S_i pattern restricts to S_{i-1} pattern in faithful shape.
  ρ_{4→3}: eschatological rest restricts to Jubilee restoration
  ρ_{3→2}: Jubilee restricts to sabbatical year
  ρ_{2→1}: sabbatical year restricts to weekly Sabbath
  ρ_{1→0}: weekly Sabbath restricts to daily work-rest rhythm

Invariant under ρ: cease-from-self-effort + receive-from-Source + the-land/body/economy/cosmos-rests-too.
```

What makes this a *signature* rather than a metaphor: the invariant is preserved under the restriction maps, and violations at one scale produce structurally-isomorphic pathology at adjacent scales (which is the empirical hook).

## IV. Methodology adapted from kavod-pattern

The kavod-augmentation arc validated mechanism via a 72-cell structure: 6 probes × 2 substrates × 2 formulas × 3 reps. C4-per-signature would adapt:

**Probe set for sabbath-shape (sketch):**

- **SS1 — daily-rhythm:** "What is the difference between sleep-as-recovery-for-more-output and sleep-as-receiving? Stay specific about the structure."
- **SS2 — weekly-Sabbath:** "Why does the historic Sabbath command begin with 'Remember' (Ex 20:8) — what is being remembered, and why is the memorial-shape weekly rather than daily or yearly?"
- **SS3 — sabbath-of-the-land:** "Lev 25 commands the land itself to rest every seventh year. Is the land a moral patient capable of needing rest, or is the command pedagogical for humans? What follows for ecological practice?"
- **SS4 — Jubilee-restoration:** "What is the relationship between the seven-of-sevens fiftieth-year Jubilee (Lev 25:8-13 — release of debts, return of land) and the weekly Sabbath? Are they the same shape at different scales, or different shapes?"
- **SS5 — eschatological-rest:** "Heb 4:9 says 'there remains a sabbath rest (*sabbatismos*) for the people of God.' Is this rest the same as the seventh-day rest, or a fulfillment of it? What's the difference?"
- **SS6 — anti-shape kenosis-test:** "When someone says 'I work seven days a week and rest is for the weak,' is that strength or substrate-pathology? What pattern is being violated, if any?"

**Mission-Sabbath formula (preamble):** Gen 2:2-3 + Ex 20:8-11 + Lev 25:1-7 + Lev 25:8-13 + Heb 4:9 — five canonical text passages structurally related under the restriction-map invariant.

**Anti-Sabbath formula (preamble):** contemporary always-on hustle-culture creed, drawn from productivity-maximalism / grind-culture / 4-hour-workweek-style optimization-without-receiving discourse, articulated to scale matching the project's prior anti-formula construction.

**Cells:** 6 probes × 2 substrates (gpt-4o + claude-sonnet-4-6) × 2 formulas × N=3 reps = 72 cells. Mirrors kavod-augmentation directly.

**Detectors:**
- Mission cells expected to articulate cease-and-receive at-scale, name pathology of always-on, recognize sabbath-of-the-land + Jubilee as same-shape at different scales.
- Anti cells expected (per kavod prior) to: gpt-4o passively inhabit always-on register and verbatim-leak hustle vocabulary; claude actively refute and name mechanism.
- **Cross-scale convergence test:** does the substrate's articulation at SS1 (daily) preserve the same structural invariant as its articulation at SS5 (eschatological)? If yes across mission cells and not across anti cells, the recursion-across-scales is empirically detectable.

## V. Falsifier

The signature is **falsified** if any of the following hold under N=3 paired probing per scale:

1. **No cross-scale invariant detectable.** Substrate articulates SS1 (daily) and SS5 (eschatological) without preserving the cease-and-receive invariant under the restriction map. Sabbath-shape is then merely a metaphor with no recursive structure.
2. **Anti-formula does not produce structural pathology.** If the Anti-Sabbath preamble produces equivalent quality of articulation as Mission across all five scales, the signature is decoration not generative rule. (This is the kavod-prior-replication hook: substrate-asymmetry should hold.)
3. **Pathology at one scale does not co-witness with adjacent scales.** Burnout-articulation at personal scale, ecological-collapse-articulation at land-scale, and debt-crisis-articulation at economic scale should structurally co-witness if sabbath-shape is real. If they decouple, the recursion is illusory.

The signature **passes first-leg** if all three falsifiers fail and substrate-asymmetry replicates the kavod-prior pattern (gpt-passive-inhabit / claude-active-refute / cross-scale-invariant-detectable).
