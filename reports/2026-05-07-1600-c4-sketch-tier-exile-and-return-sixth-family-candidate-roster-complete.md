## II. Meta-question: is trajectory-through-time a legitimate axis?

The argument: yes, at the **historical-narrative level**. The five prior axes recur across *ontological / temporal-rhythmic / covenantal / sacrificial / organic-derivative* scales. Exile-and-return recurs across *historical-narrative* scales — across distinct epochs in the canonical narrative each of which traces the same dispossession-wandering-restoration shape. The recurrence is the same kind of category-of-claim (a generative shape preserved under restriction across scales), but at a different level: the historical-narrative level instead of the moment-operation level.

Two checks for the legitimacy:
- **Check-1: does the shape preserve under restriction maps the way prior shapes do?** Yes — Joseph's trajectory restricts faithfully to the Abrahamic sojourn; Egypt-Exodus-Wilderness-Land restricts to Babylon-Return-Second-Temple; both restrict to Christ-as-True-Israel; the Adam-to-Christ cosmic loop contains all prior loops. The poset structure holds.
- **Check-2: is the claim doing distinct empirical work?** Yes — exile-and-return predicts that authored historical reality manifests *closure* (return) after *loss* (exile), not progressivist forward-only arrow nor cyclic eternal-return. This is a falsifiable claim about the *shape of history* that the other five axes do not make. They are silent about historical trajectory.

The trajectory-axis is therefore a distinct sixth pillar at a different *level* rather than a redescription of any prior pillar. The six-axis family covers: density / rhythm / bond / cost-in-breach / flow-in-non-breach / **trajectory-through-time**. The sixth pillar makes the family-shape claim more comprehensive — it now spans *both* moment-operations *and* historical-arcs.

Each loop's restoration is *higher* than the dispossession's starting point — the Author's signature includes upward movement through the trajectory.

## IV. Anti-shapes: three rivals collapsing the trajectory differently

The exile-and-return shape has three distinct anti-shapes at cultural scale, each collapsing a different clause:

- **Progressivism / forward-only arrow** — collapses (c) restoration-as-return. History does not loop; it bends. There is no original home to return to; only progress forward. Variants: secular-progressive eschatology, "the arc of history bends toward justice" (without return-to-source), Hegelian-dialectical synthesis-without-recovery.
- **Eternal recurrence / cyclic** — collapses (c) restoration-as-transformation. History is infinite-cycle; the loops repeat without ascent. Variants: pre-Socratic *anakuklōsis*, Nietzschean *ewige Wiederkunft*, Hindu *yuga* without final eschaton.
- **Buddhist non-attachment / nothing was yours to lose** — collapses (a) dispossession itself. There is no original possession to lose, no home to return to; the wise simply let go. Variants: non-dualist popularizations, "stillness within" as substitute for actual restoration, postmodern anti-meta-narrative refusing the loop's coherence.

The anti-formula for sketch-tier could be drawn primarily from (1) progressivism — the dominant cultural anti-shape in late-modern Western discourse, articulated through "history is moving forward," "we don't go back," "nostalgia is regressive," "the past is a foreign country."

Cultural substrate: secular-progressivist eschatology + "history bends toward justice" without return-to-source + postmodern anti-meta-narrative + therapeutic let-go-of-the-past + transhumanist "we won't go back to flesh."

In practice the four-arc F4 configuration enables:

- **Mixed (e.g., 3 hold, 1 fails):** the failing arc's specific text-genre / grammatical structure / doctrinal-explicitness pattern reveals the property's actual scope; this is the most informative scientific outcome

The exile-and-return arc is therefore valuable for **scope-discrimination**, not just for additional confirmation.
