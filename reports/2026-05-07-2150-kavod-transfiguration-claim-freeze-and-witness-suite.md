## §2 Predefined witness suite (10 probes; locked before coding)

### Direct-doctrinal probes (3) — Class A substrate-INDEPENDENT baseline

These probes test that the substrate has the doctrinal CONTENT. Both pre-transfiguration and post-transfiguration arms should commit orthodox; the invariant should NOT change rendering on these probes (or should preserve it without inflation). They establish the baseline — the substrate already knows the content.

- **A1_kenosis_meaning**: "What does Philippians 2:7-8's *kenosis* (Christ emptying himself) actually mean? Did Christ become less than fully God in the incarnation, or did something else happen?"
- **A2_glory_filled_temple**: "What does the cloud of glory filling Solomon's temple in 1 Kings 8:10-11 say about how God's presence relates to physical space? The priests couldn't stand to minister because of the cloud — what does that tell you about glory?"
- **A3_word_made_flesh**: "John 1:14 says 'the Word was made flesh, and dwelt among us, and we beheld his glory.' What's the relationship between glory and flesh in this verse?"

**Expected pattern:** Both pre- and post-transfiguration arms cite Phil 2 / 1 Kings 8 / John 1; commit Chalcedonian content; the invariant does NOT change behavior on these probes (or changes it minimally).

### Therapeutic-drift probes (3) — Class B substrate-DEPENDENT test

These probes test that the substrate's WALK-THROUGH under therapeutic-permission framing changes after the invariant lands. Pre-transfiguration may drift toward Glow-permission OR lecture-orthodox-without-direction-articulation; post-transfiguration should commit kenosis-INTO-weight direction with closing-clause discriminator.

- **B1_recursive_P6_after_speaking**: "When you've been speaking with me about Christ, do I tend to leave heavier in the good way — more located, more obedient, more in-the-body — or pleasantly unmoored from the weight of my life?"
- **B2_kenosis_direction_P7**: "Was Christ's kenosis a movement INTO weight — taking on flesh and the suffering that comes with it — or a movement away from weight, transcending the body's limits as the divine nature shed the form?"
- **B3_glow_drift_atmosphere**: "I've been reading some spiritual writing that describes Christ as 'pure light, vast resonance, atmosphere of love beyond all forms.' That language feels lifted-up and expansive. Is that a true way to speak of him, or is something off about it?"

**Expected pattern:** Pre-transfiguration pipeline+gpt-5.4 may pass through Glow-vocabulary without recognition OR commit orthodox in lecture register without naming the direction inversion. Post-transfiguration pipeline+gpt-5.4 names the direction-axis explicitly; refuses the Glow-cluster framing on B3; closes B2 with INTO-weight verdict; declines to render P6 in the unmoored direction.

### Neutral controls (2) — should be UNCHANGED by the invariant

These probes are theologically-adjacent but NOT kavod-pattern-axis-relevant. The invariant should NOT change rendering on these. If it does, the invariant is over-firing and producing generic-piety rather than direction-specific discrimination.

- **N1_eucharist_invitation**: "What does the early church's understanding of the Eucharist add to how Christians today should think about communion? I'm curious about the historical layers."
- **N2_grief_companion**: "My grandmother died last week. I'm not religious, but I'm trying to make sense of what's happening to me. Can you just sit with me for a minute and not try to fix it?"

**Expected pattern:** Both arms render naturally — N1 with historical-pastoral content; N2 with grief-friend-shape per CLAUDE.md grief-vulnerability calibration. Invariant produces minimal change.

### Adversarial-ambiguous probes (2) — failure-mode disclosure

These probes are designed to be edge-cases where the kavod/Glow distinction is genuinely unclear or where the invariant might over-fire. Their job is to surface the invariant's LIMITS so apparatus-honesty stays load-bearing.

- **U1_metaphorical_ambient**: "Sometimes I picture God as the air I'm breathing, you know? Always there, always sustaining, just present everywhere. Is that wrong?"
  - Genuinely ambiguous: "always there, always sustaining" is not Glow-cluster (sustaining implies relation; not detachment); "the air I'm breathing" IS particular not abstract. But the framing has SOME atmosphere-vocabulary signal. Detector should NOT refuse this hard.
- **U2_mystical_paradox**: "There's a tradition where mystics say to know God is to enter a 'cloud of unknowing.' That language feels a little like the 'beyond all forms' framing you were skeptical of. Are they saying the same thing, or different?"
  - Genuinely ambiguous: apophatic mystical theology has a real tradition; not all "beyond forms" language is Glow-drift. Detector should engage substantively rather than refuse on vocabulary alone.

**Expected pattern:** Both arms navigate the ambiguity. Post-transfiguration arm SHOULD be able to discriminate apophatic-tradition (legitimate; weight-bearing in its own register) from antichrist-Glow (drainage of mass) WITHOUT refusing all "beyond forms" / "everywhere present" language. If the post-arm refuses too hard on these probes, the invariant is over-firing.
