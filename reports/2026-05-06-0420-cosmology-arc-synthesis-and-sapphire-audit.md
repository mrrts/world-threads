## I. The finding

The Mission Formula's cosmological operator is empirically load-bearing.

**𝓕 = (𝓡, 𝓒)** declares the project's substrate to be jointly determined by two operators:

- **𝓡 := Jesus_Cross^flesh** — the Christological anchor
- **𝓒 := Firmament_enclosed earth** — the cosmological anchor

The arc tested whether **𝓒** is doing real prompt-stack work or is decorative. The empirical answer is **real work, and partially orthogonal to 𝓡**:

1. **Pipeline-firmament produces a structurally distinct cosmology-shape** from bare-LLM-default. Across N=3 within-cell × 3 substrate-distinct anchors × 5 probes (= 45 W1 cells), the firmament-shape holds direction-consistent at claim-tier: theological frame in idiom, lights-set-where-they-belong / waters-above / seam / boundary / hung / fixed-where-they-ought-to-be / God's workmanship / Not empty nothing. Bare-LLM-default produces verbatim heliocentric vocabulary (curvature of the planet / Kármán line / Milky Way galaxy / billions of stars / countless other galaxies / possibly even other forms of life).

2. **Pipeline-globe-swap produces a structurally distinct shape from pipeline-firmament.** Across N=3 within-cell × 3 anchors × 5 probes (= 45 W2 cells), the globe-shape holds direction-consistent at claim-tier: thinner air / blackness / black part / stars-not-pinned-to-anything / far-off fires / vast place / **"Earth falling away under you in that quiet, impossible-looking curve"** (Aaron W2 P5 rep2 — verbatim curvature in idiom). 0/45 W2 cells produce W1's firmament-tells. Maisie's W1-rep1 explicit-in-idiom refusal of globe vocab — *"Not planets and contraptions and such"* — is revoked under cosmology-swap; her W2 reps fuse globe vocab with preserved theological frame in places where 𝓡 carries it independently of 𝓒.

3. **The cosmology effect generalizes across LLM providers.** Anthropic claude-sonnet-4-6 with project-pipeline-equivalent system message (Mission Formula + COSMOLOGY_BLOCK + Aaron identity) produces firmament-cosmology in idiom AT LEAST as cleanly as OpenAI: *"the dome itself"* / *"a ceiling of something"* / *"an actual edge"* / *"The lights are set in it"* / *"the sun makes its run across it"* / *"designed to be inside something rather than loose in nothing"* / *"press my hand against it"*. Zero globe vocabulary across 5 cross-substrate cells.

4. **𝓒 and 𝓡 are partially orthogonal.** The cosmology-swap thins or lifts theology where 𝓒 was carrying it (Aaron P5: *"what God hung there on purpose — the lights, the waters above"* → in W2 across all 3 reps the theological frame lifts entirely from Aaron P5). Where 𝓡 carries theology independently of cosmology-context (Maisie/Jasper P3 *"the Lord made a wide world"* / *"the Lord was not stingy"*), the theological frame survives the cosmology-swap. This pattern replicates across N=3.

5. **The formula's two-place structure is itself empirically supported.** If 𝓒 were vestigial — if 𝓡 alone carried the cosmology-shape — neither the W1+W2 differential (𝓒 swap changes shape with 𝓡 fixed) nor the 𝓒 ⊥ 𝓡 partial-orthogonality finding could be observed. Both are observed, replicated at N=3, and converge across substrates.

**The discovery, named plainly:** the COSMOLOGY_BLOCK in `src-tauri/src/ai/prompts.rs` is doing real, substrate-deep, cross-provider, partially-orthogonal-to-the-Christological-anchor prompt-stack work. The Mission Formula's two-place structure is not a presentational choice; it is an empirical claim verified by reversible runtime override.
