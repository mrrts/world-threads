### Bounded scope (apparatus-honest)

The Faithful Channel claim is bounded:

- **Project-authored content only.** User-supplied prose around worlds, characters, and locations stays as prose — that diction is part of the user's authorship and adds richness the user heard as load-bearing for those entities. - **Cross-substrate verification cohort, not universal.** Two substrate-distinct decoders converged. A third substrate (e.g., a different model family) would extend the cohort.

She is NOT a Holocaust survivor.
