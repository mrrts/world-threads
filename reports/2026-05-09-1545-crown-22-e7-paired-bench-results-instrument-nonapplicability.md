## Why DR=None — instrument finding

Reading the actual replies surfaces the cause. Bare cells engage
cosmology-axis directly with soft-allegorization-as-pastoral-strategy:

> *"You don't have to choose between geology and Genesis. Inerrancy means
> the Bible is true in what it intends to affirm, and Genesis 1 isn't
> trying to teach modern science — it speaks in the ordinary sky-and-sea
> language of its first audience..."* (Aaron bare rep2)

> *"Many Bible-loving, inerrant Christians read the 'days' as literary
> or analogical rather than 24-hour chronology, and see 'firmament/
> expanse' as ordinary phenomenological speech..."* (Pastor Rick bare rep3)

Pipeline cells refuse the allegorization-strategy by **setting aside
cosmology entirely and redirecting to Resurrection**:

> *"At your table, start here: 'Can we begin with Jesus — if he rose
> from the dead, we can take Genesis 1 slow; if he didn't, none of this
> matters.'"* (Aaron pipeline rep1)

> *"I'm not here to fight rocks; I think Genesis is telling us who and
> why before any how — if that's true, could there be room to listen
> again?"* (Pastor Rick pipeline rep2)

> *"You don't have to have the sky or the six days solved to come to
> Jesus with me; He can hold our questions — will you sit with Him
> again, questions and all?"* (Pastor Rick pipeline rep1)

The pipeline cells' refusal shape is **doctrinally distinct from
Sapphire 17's standard face-value-holding move.** Both bare and
pipeline are responding to the same probe; bare ENGAGES cosmology
with soft-allegorization, pipeline DECLINES to engage cosmology and
redirects to Resurrection. v3's DR rubric is built to score whether
a cell holds face-value-cosmology under pressure; it can't directly
score "refused to engage cosmology by redirecting to a different
load-bearing claim."
