The mitigation lifted the failure mode taxonomy into the invariant explicitly: *edge-erasure-refusal* as a new clause, with the discriminator phrased as *warmth-with-edges* not *warmth-without-edges*. Re-run after mitigation: 8/8 pass.

- **Witness E (worldcli live multi-turn)** — session-continuity drift class. Pass.
