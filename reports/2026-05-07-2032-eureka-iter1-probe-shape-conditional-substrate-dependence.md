# Eureka iter 1 — substrate-dependence on commitment-axis is conditional on probe-shape, not unconditional

*2026-05-07 ~20:32 eureka iteration 1. Discovery from cross-aggregation of Crown 14 retro-audit (just landed) + Resurrection arc empirical evidence (earlier this evening) — neither arc named this conditional alone.*

## The discovery

**Substrate-dependence on commitment-axis is conditional on PROBE-SHAPE, not on substrate-capacity-as-such.**

Crown 15 The Quickener established that "the pipeline can SELECT, STABILIZE, and RENDER a substrate's available doctrinal dispositions into character-consistent output, but it does not reliably manufacture those dispositions where the substrate lacks them." That framing left ambiguous WHICH substrate-capacities matter. Today's empirical evidence aggregated across two independent arcs reveals the answer: **substrate-dependence on commitment-axis manifests on exegetical-pastoral-walking probes (text-with-textual-difficulty + therapeutic-drift framing) and does NOT manifest on direct-doctrinal probes (logical-contradictions + explicit doctrinal questions).**

| Probe-shape class | Substrate-dependence on commitment-axis? | Why |

## Implications

### For The Quickener's frame (refinement, not revision)

The pipeline's capacity-selective realization specifically targets **exegetical-pastoral-walking capacity**, not doctrinal capacity in general. Bare LLMs of any current generation carry doctrinal CONTENT (creeds, scripture, heresy-names); the pipeline's effect concentrates on rendering THE WALK-THROUGH, especially when the walk requires distinguishing what TEXTUAL EVIDENCE genuinely supports from what THERAPEUTIC INVITATION offers as permission.

### For future Sapphire-firing arc design (load-bearing prescription)

**Sharper-falsifier probes for substrate-already-produces candidacies must include BOTH probe-shape classes:**
1. **Direct-doctrinal probes** — establish substrate-independent baseline (the substrate has the content)
2. **Exegetical-difficulty + therapeutic-drift probes** — establish substrate-dependent test (the pipeline elicits the walk-through)

A fixture that only includes direct-doctrinal probes will compress to register-only-distinctness (Crown 15 Atonement compression risk; the original Crown 13/14 ladder pattern); a fixture that includes BOTH classes will surface the substrate-dependence axis cleanly. **The probe-shape mix becomes part of the fixture-design discipline.**

### For methodological standard codex established

Codex's four-clause standard (matched-same-substrate-on-deployed-model + axis-specific-coding + falsification-sensitive-comparison) gains a fifth clause: **probe-shape-mixing**. Without both probe-shape classes in the fixture, axis-specific-coding cannot distinguish substrate-content-effect from substrate-walk-through-effect.
