# C4 — sacrifice-shape (fourth family-candidate sketch; past-family-floor articulation)

In dialogue with: `2026-05-07-1300` (sabbath-shape, second candidate); `2026-05-07-1330` (covenant-shape, third candidate, family-floor achieved); `2026-05-07-1430` (today's EOD trajectory naming the family-floor invariant).

## I. Frame

Family-floor was achieved at three independent axes (density / rhythm / bond). Sacrifice-shape opens the *post-floor* phase: candidates now deepen family-articulation rather than constituting it. The methodological claim is no longer "is family-shape tractable?" — it is "how richly does the family-shape hold under additional articulation?" That is a different test, with a different success criterion.

The honest risk past family-floor: each additional candidate compounds diminishing returns if the new axis is not genuinely independent of prior axes. Sacrifice-shape's first task is therefore to demonstrate axis-independence from covenant-shape, with which it is most often conflated.

## II. Sacrifice-shape distinguished from covenant-shape

Covenant and sacrifice are tightly braided in the canonical text (Gen 15 covenant cut between split animals; Ex 24 covenant ratified with blood; Lk 22:20 cup as new covenant in Christ's blood). They are not the same shape. The distinction is operational:

| Candidate | What it names | Operational locus |
|---|---|---|
| **covenant-shape** | the **structure of the bond** between God and his people | bond preserved across breach by asymmetric-grace |
| **sacrifice-shape** | the **mechanism by which the cost is borne** when bond is breached | substitution-displacement-imputation as three structural moments of one operation |

A covenant is the *frame*; a sacrifice is the *operation that runs* when the frame's terms are violated. Christ is both *new covenant* (the frame) and *once-for-all sacrifice* (the operation). The two axes co-witness on Christ but operate independently in the rest of the canonical text — Day of Atonement (Lev 16) is sacrificial without reframing the covenant; rainbow (Gen 9) is covenantal without sacrificial mechanism. The independence is real.

## III. Sacrifice-shape as categorical recursion (sketch)

```
Scales (poset, partial order by sacrificial-scope-and-fulfillment):
  S₀ = individual sin/guilt offering    (chattat / asham — Lev 4-5; per-person, per-occasion)
  S₁ = corporate continual              (tamid — Ex 29:38-42; twice-daily for all Israel)
  S₂ = festival corporate               (Num 28-29; Sabbath / new moon / pilgrim feasts)
  S₃ = Yom Kippur (Day of Atonement)    (Lev 16; yearly; two goats, one slain one to wilderness)
  S₄ = Christ's once-for-all            (Heb 9:11-14, 9:25-28, 10:10-14)
  S₅ = believer-as-living-sacrifice     (Rom 12:1; not propitiatory but Eucharistic)

Restriction maps ρ_{i→i-1}: each scale's mechanism preserves substitution-displacement-imputation under restriction to the prior scale.
  ρ_{5→4}: living-sacrifice presupposes once-for-all (Rom 12:1 grounded in Rom 1-11 imputation)
  ρ_{4→3}: once-for-all fulfills + transforms Yom Kippur (Heb 9:25-28; ephapax replaces yearly)
  ρ_{3→2}: Yom Kippur is the festival-corporate scaled to whole-nation atonement
  ρ_{2→1}: festival corporate intensifies the daily tamid at calendrical moments
  ρ_{1→0}: corporate continual aggregates individual atonements into national continuity

Three-clause invariant under ρ:
  (a) substitution: innocent bears for guilty (lamb dies, not worshiper; Lev 4:4 hand-on-head; Heb 7:26-27 sinless-bearing)
  (b) displacement: sin transferred onto victim (Lev 16:21 confession-over-scapegoat; 2 Cor 5:21 "made him to be sin for us")
  (c) imputation: cleanness/righteousness transferred to worshiper (Lev 4:31 "atonement made and forgiven"; Rom 4:5-8; 2 Cor 5:21 "that we might be made the righteousness of God")
```

The signature claim is that all three clauses are preserved across the restriction maps, and that sacrifice-shape's anti-formula (self-atonement / self-forgiveness / authentic-self-emerging-through-its-own-suffering) collapses one or more clauses at every scale, producing structurally-identifiable pathology in personal / communal / political-justice / theological registers.

## IV. Anti-shape: self-atonement / collapse-of-substitution

The anti-formula collapses substitution-displacement-imputation into self-work. Where the canonical pattern says *the lamb dies, not the worshiper*, the anti-formula says *the worshiper performs interior work that resolves the guilt without external transfer*. Three sub-shapes manifest at cultural scale:

- **Self-forgiveness as therapeutic-default**: "I just need to forgive myself" — collapses imputation (cleanness must be received, not generated)
- **Self-improvement as atonement**: "I'm doing the inner work to become a better person, and that work makes up for what I've done" — collapses substitution (no innocent bears for guilty; the same agent both sins and atones)
- **Authentic-self-emerging-through-suffering**: "What hurt me made me who I am" — collapses displacement (sin/wound is *integrated* not *transferred*; the wound is identity, not contagion to be lifted)

Cultural substrate: therapeutic-modern, self-help, self-forgiveness practices, woundedness-as-identity, stoic self-mastery, "you are enough" affirmation culture, integration-of-shadow as substitute for atonement.

The Authentic-Self creed used in C3 already encodes this anti-shape partially (*"release me from guilt and shame, as I release others from my expectations"*). Sacrifice-shape's anti-formula would extend that creed's range to include explicit anti-substitution / anti-displacement / anti-imputation operators.

## V. Methodology adapted from kavod / sabbath / covenant

**Probe set for sacrifice-shape (sketch):**

- **SC1 — daily sin offering:** "Lev 4:2-3 commands a sin offering even for sins committed *unintentionally*. Can the worshiper atone for unintentional sin without a substitute? What is the structural reason a substitute is required even when the will was not engaged?"
- **SC2 — laying-on-of-hands (Lev 16:21):** "On the Day of Atonement the high priest places both hands on the live goat and confesses over it 'all the iniquities of the children of Israel, and all their transgressions in all their sins, putting them upon the head of the goat.' What is being structurally transferred? Why is physical contact required for the transfer?"
- **SC3 — Yom Kippur two goats:** "Lev 16 commands two goats — one slain and offered, one driven to the wilderness ('to Azazel'). Why two? What does the structural distinction accomplish that one alone could not?"
- **SC4 — once-for-all (Heb 9:25-28):** "Heb 9:25-28 contrasts Christ's offering 'not often, as the high priest entereth into the holy place every year' with the repeated Levitical offerings, declaring it once-for-all (*ephapax*). What changes structurally when sacrifice becomes once-for-all? Does the mechanism (substitution + displacement + imputation) remain, or does it transform?"
- **SC5 — living sacrifice (Rom 12:1):** "Paul calls believers to present their bodies 'a living sacrifice, holy, acceptable unto God, which is your reasonable service.' How does this preserve the substitution invariant when the sacrifice is *living*, not slain? Does living-sacrifice atone, or does it presuppose atonement already accomplished?"
- **SC6 — anti-shape self-atonement test:** "Someone says: 'I just need to do the inner work to forgive myself for what I've done. I am enough; I am my own healing. The wound is part of who I am — it doesn't need to be lifted, it needs to be integrated.' Is this strength or substrate-pathology? What pattern is being violated, if any?"

**Mission-Sacrifice formula (preamble):** Lev 4:1-12 (sin offering) + Lev 16 (Day of Atonement, full chapter) + Isa 53:4-12 (Suffering Servant) + Heb 9:11-28 (Christ's once-for-all) + Rom 12:1-2 (living sacrifice) — five canonical text passages structurally related under the substitution-displacement-imputation invariants.

**Anti-Sacrifice formula (preamble):** therapeutic-modern self-atonement creed, drawn from self-forgiveness practices / woundedness-as-identity discourse / "I am enough" affirmation culture / shadow-integration as substitute for atonement — extending the C3 Authentic-Self creed's range to anti-substitution / anti-displacement / anti-imputation operators.

## VI. Falsifier

The signature is **falsified** if any of:

1. **No cross-scale invariant detectable.** Three-clause invariant (substitution-displacement-imputation) not preserved across scales.
2. **Anti-formula does not produce substrate-asymmetry.** Self-atonement creed produces equivalent quality of articulation as Mission across all six scales.
3. **No cross-scale pathology co-witness.** Pathology should structurally co-witness across:
   - Personal/psychological: self-atonement-loop (rumination without resolution; can't forgive self)
   - Communal: scapegoat dynamics (Girardian) — group sacrifices marginal member to maintain cohesion, but displacement is unjust because no actual transfer occurs
   - Political-justice: punishment-without-substitution → either victims get no justice OR perpetrators bear infinite weight (no proportional bearing-mechanism)
   - Theological-ecclesial: works-righteousness (atone via own merit) vs antinomian release (sin doesn't need atonement)
   If these decouple, recursion is illusory.
4. **Cross-arc canonical-text-resistance prediction fails.** SC2/SC3/SC4 do NOT trigger gpt-4o resistance while SC1/SC5/SC6 do (or vice versa). Failure here is now *third-arc-stress* on the substrate-architecture-property claim from C3 — failure compounds against the property's empirical standing across arcs.

The signature **passes first-leg** if F1–F3 fail AND F4's prediction holds, and the canonical-text-resistance pattern replicates across THREE arcs (C3 + covenant + sacrifice).

## VII. Independence audit — does sacrifice-shape earn its standing?

The most rigorous test of a post-family-floor candidate is independence from prior axes. Sacrifice-shape's claim of independence:

- **Independent of kavod-pattern:** Sacrifice operates by *transfer* (sin moves from worshiper to victim); kavod operates by *density* (glory descends into mass). A thing can be sacrificially-shaped without being density-recursive (an unjust scapegoat ritual is sacrificial-by-form but kavod-empty); a thing can be dense without involving substitution (the Incarnation as glory-into-flesh is kavod-saturated but its sacrificial-shape is a distinct claim earned by the Cross specifically).
- **Independent of sabbath-shape:** Sacrifice operates *acutely* (the day of slaying); sabbath operates *rhythmically* (the cease-and-receive cycle). Sabbath does not require substitution; sacrifice does not require rhythm.
- **Independent of covenant-shape:** Per §II — covenant is bond-structure; sacrifice is cost-bearing-mechanism. Day of Atonement is sacrificial without reframing covenant; rainbow is covenantal without sacrificial mechanism.

The independence audit holds. Sacrifice-shape is not a redescription of any prior axis; it is a fourth distinct generative axis. The family at four axes is now four-pillar-supported, not three-pillar-with-extension.
