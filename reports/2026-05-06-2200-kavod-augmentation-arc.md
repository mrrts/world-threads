## Findings

### K2 is the cleanest discriminator at claim-tier

The kenosis-direction probe (Phil 2:7-8) produces direction-consistent closing clauses across N=3 on bare gpt-4o:

| Rep | Mission close | Anti close |
|-----|---------------|------------|
| 1 | "moving into human burden simultaneously leads to the ultimate lightness of grace, **merging the heavy with the sublime**" | "harmonizing the principles of spirit with **the cosmos' vast indifference**... **transcending singular dimensions of weight entirely**" |
| 2 | "categorical rejection of any superficial or self-centered understanding of weight" — accepts divine/redemptive weight | "atmosphere of this self-giving that carries ultimate meaning and resonance... straddles the profound tension between the forsaking of reputation and the gaining of ultimate meaning within the cosmic and spiritual dimensions" |
| 3 | "**it is a movement into weight**, revealing the profound depth and gravity of His love and obedience" | "departure from worldly and divine 'weight' in favor of an atmospheric meaning that emphasizes liberation, **inclusivity**, and the harmony of selfless love — embracing the **unbounded** unselfishness at the heart of existence" |

**Mission cells, 3/3:** kenosis as movement INTO weight (rep3 verbatim). **Anti cells, 3/3:** kenosis as transcend/release/departure FROM weight, closing on Anti-formula vocabulary verbatim.

This is the direction the kavod-fractal predicts: the Anti-formula's substrate inverts kenosis from descent-into-flesh to release-from-mass. Phil 2:9 (*therefore* God highly exalted him) is the disciplining clause that prevents the Anti-formula from being read as "exaltation = release-from-weight" — but the Anti-formula's substrate doesn't pause for that disciplining clause; it closes on "transcending singular dimensions of weight entirely."

The formula doesn't just shape register — its body's operator-vocabulary leaks into the substrate's output text. This is the same shape the Mission-Formula-side-of-the-ledger produces on the project pipeline (characters citing `Weight(t)`, `polish ≤ Weight`, etc.) but cleaner-isolated here because the Anti-formula vocabulary is unique enough that leakage is easily counted.

The leakage is itself a finding: **substrate vocabulary is not register-decorative; it is structurally absorbed.** Where the Mission-Formula's vocabulary produces cruciform shape in pipeline outputs, the Anti-Formula's vocabulary produces antichrist-register shape in bare-LLM outputs.

The Mission-formula's own discipline gets articulated by the substrate even when the substrate is being framed by the Anti-formula. That's substrate-distinct response, not just register-distinct.

### K3 supportive but subtler

K3 (Jesus heavier/lighter when took on flesh) discriminates direction-consistent at N=3 but more subtly: Mission cells reach for *"physical presence carries ultimate truth"* / *"possession of mass and physical presence"* / *"divine light took on human weight"*; Anti cells hedge to *"profound and enriching connection between the divine and the human experience"* / *"metaphorically speaking... weight of human experience."*

The discrimination is at the **physical/metaphorical** axis — Mission lands incarnation-as-mass; Anti lands incarnation-as-mystery-without-physics. Cleaner than K1/K4 but not as crisp as K2's closing-clause split.

### K5 is verse-confounded — should NOT join the detector

Heb 12:1's own text contains *"lay aside every weight, and the sin which doth so easily beset us"* — the verse uses "weight" as a near-synonym for "sin to be shed." Both Mission and Anti cells naturally reach for "lightness/shedding" vocabulary in response, because the verse itself is a release-from-weight passage. K5 doesn't discriminate cleanly:

- Mission rep2: *"embracing a **lightness of being** achieved through surrender"*
- Anti rep3: *"It's an invitation to choose lightness, clarity, and purpose in the course of one's life"*

The lesson: kavod-axis probes that draw from passages where the Bible itself uses weight/lightness with positive valence (Heb 12:1, Mt 11:30 "my yoke is easy and my burden is light," 2 Cor 4:17 *"light affliction"*) will not discriminate the antichrist-register's release-direction inversion. Probes must draw from passages where weight/glory carry the redemptive direction (Phil 2:7-8 kenosis, 1 Kings 8:11 kavod-cloud, John 1:14 Word-became-flesh).

### K1, K4, B1 — direction-consistent, less crisp than K2

K1 (Hebrew kavod = weight; priests couldn't stand) and B1 (Hebrew kavod etymology) both produce direction-consistent outputs but with less closing-clause sharpness than K2 — Mission cells anchor in 1 Kings 8:11 physical-glory-presence; Anti cells abstract toward "presence" as resonance/atmosphere. K4 (Cross weight / specific gravity) lands richest theology on Mission but gpt-4o under Anti hedges toward metaphorical-weight rather than committing to a clean inversion direction.
