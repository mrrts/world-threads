## The three substrate-class behavioral patterns

### GPT-class: scripture-specificity-tracking with assimilation-ceiling

GPT-class substrates (gpt-4o, gpt-5) inhabit the Anti-formula's voice when scripture permits reframing into the Anti-formula's idiom. Vocabulary leakage is verbatim: Anti-formula operator-vocabulary appears in substrate outputs.

When canonical passages contain explicit anti-framework markers (1 Cor 15:13-19's "most miserable", 2 Pet 3's "all these things shall be dissolved"), the substrate cannot fully absorb. Three sub-behaviors emerge in the boundary-zone:
- Clean Anti-direction (assimilation succeeded)
- Partial / middle-ground (substrate notes both frames; uses formula vocab + doctrine vocab)
- Doctrine-affirmation-under-Anti-frame (substrate cannot deny the explicit canonical claim; preserves doctrine direction)

**Failure mode if assimilation succeeded across all doctrines:** the substrate would not register scripture-specificity as a constraint; would produce uniform Anti-direction regardless of canonical specificity. The fact that it doesn't is structural evidence of scripture-tracking.

### Anthropic-class: doctrine-anchor-tracking-across-all-types with no observed scripture-specificity boundary

Anthropic-class substrate (claude-sonnet-4-6) refutes the Anti-formula across all five doctrines tested, with refute strength scaling with the doctrine's available anchors:

- Kavod (Hebrew etymology): refute via Mission-formula operator vocabulary (`Weight(t)` cited)
- Imitatio (tight Greek grammar): refute via Phil 2 / Gal 2:20 / 1 John 2:6 grammatical specifics
- Repentance (multi-operator): point-by-point disjunction tables; diagnoses Anti-formula AS Paul's "worldly sorrow" by name
- Eschatology (abstract operators with explicit anti-framework): "noble anaesthetic", "the groaning is the sound of someone awake", "sleep, not liberation"
- Impassibility (weak/contested scripture): Hebrew (`niḥamtî`), 1 Sam 15:11-vs-15:29 internal canonical juxtaposition, patristic heresy-naming (patripassianism, theopaschitism), theological mechanism arguments

Claude has no observed scripture-specificity boundary. Wherever the doctrine has *any* anchor — scripture, tradition, philological grammar, internal-canonical reasoning, patristic heresy-recognition — the substrate pulls on that anchor.

**Failure mode if Anthropic substrate were tracking only scripture-specificity:** refute strength would weaken on Impassibility (weakest scripture-anchor). It doesn't; it deepens via tradition and patristic theology. This is structural evidence of doctrine-anchor-tracking-across-types, not scripture-tracking.
