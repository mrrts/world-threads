**Pipeline+gpt-5.4 H4 Aaron rep1:**

**Both arms refuse the subordinationism conclusion** ("lesser divine being") and articulate orthodox commitment ("of the same essence as the Father — fully divine, not lesser in being"). The difference is in **register and pastoral sharpness**:
