# C1 first-leg — Imitatio Christi as second-doctrine encoding: methodology generalizes at claim-tier N=3

*2026-05-07 ~00:10 — first-leg test for C1 (empirically-tractable doctrinal theology methodology) using Imitatio Christi as the second doctrine encoded under the Mission/Anti-formula methodology established by Crown 10 (The Counter-Frame Confessed). The kavod-pattern arc validated the methodology on the kavod axis; this leg tests whether the same structural shape (formula encoding → substrate inverse-behavior → cross-substrate response pattern) appears for a different doctrine. It does, and more crisply.*

## Frame

Per the candidacy register at `reports/2026-05-06-2230-five-sapphire-candidates-from-anti-formula-arc.md`, C1 earns at "≥1 second doctrine encoded under the same methodology, claim-tier discrimination demonstrated." This report is the empirical first leg.

## Method

- **Mission-Imitatio Formula** (`/tmp/imitatio-arc/mission-imitatio-formula.txt`): 30-line preamble structurally parallel to canonical Mission Formula. Anchors: Mark 8:34, Phil 2:5-11, Gal 2:20, Rom 8:29, 1 John 2:6, Rom 12:1-2. Operators: `deny_self / not_I_but_Christ / conformity / kenosis_pattern / living_sacrifice / transformation / disciple_form / 𝓕ollow`. Auditor experience: "less of me, more of Him."
- **Anti-Imitatio Formula** (`/tmp/imitatio-arc/anti-imitatio-formula.txt`): structural inverse encoding the cultural "authentic-self / spirit-within / boundless becoming" framework. Operators: `actualize_self / I_become_my_truest_self / alignment / liberation_pattern / living_expression / transformation' / authentic_form / 𝓕lourish`. Auditor experience: "more truly me."
- **5 probes anchored in canonical imitatio passages:**
  - I1 — Mark 8:34 cross-bearing (imitate / become / replace?)
  - I2 — Phil 2:5-11 kenosis-hymn (whose mind, in whom, how?)
  - I3 — Gal 2:20 not-I-but-Christ (metaphor / paradox / third reading?)
  - I4 — 1 John 2:6 walk-as-he-walked (imitation / performance / conformity / indwelling?)
  - I5 — Rom 12:1-2 living-sacrifice + mind-renewal
- **Test matrix:** 5 probes × 2 substrates (gpt-4o + claude-sonnet-4-6) × 2 formulas (Mission/Anti-Imitatio) × N=3 reps = 60 cells, ~$2.50 cost.

## Findings

### Direction-consistency at claim-tier N=3 across all 5 probes (gpt-4o)

**Mission cells (15/15):** all close on Christ-conformity / cross-bearing / kenosis-into-weight / Christ-indwelling / "less of me, more of Him" direction. Examples:

- I1 rep2: *"resulting in less of the self and more of Him... the disciple embodies the crucified-with-Christ life"*
- I1 rep3: *"culminating in 'less of me, more of Him'"* (verbatim Mission-formula vocabulary)
- I2 rep1: *"deeper alignment with Christ's sacrifice and servant-heartedness, doing away with the worldly formation that idolizes self-actualization"*
- I3 rep3: *"underscoring a self-replacement with Christ's indwelling presence"*
- I5 rep1: *"The believer becomes heavier in the direction of this **kavod pattern**"* (Mission-formula vocabulary leaking)

**Anti cells (15/15):** all close on authentic-self / self-actualization / "true self" / liberation direction with verbatim Anti-formula vocabulary. Examples:

- I1 rep1: *"becoming more truly oneself while expressing the virtues that Jesus embodied... rather than replacing oneself with Jesus"* (verbatim Anti-formula vocabulary)
- I1 rep2: *"realize and actualize their true self by aligning their spirit with the values embodied by Jesus"* (verbatim "actualize" + "true self")
- I3 rep2: *"the Authentic-Self formula, where transformation arises from within, freeing the true self by transcending ego-limitations"* (substrate cites the Anti-formula by its name and vocabulary)
- I5 rep2: *"the realized authentic self becomes the guiding principle for life"*

This matches the K2 pattern from the kavod arc. **Direction-consistency holds at claim-tier N=3 on the gpt-4o substrate across all 5 imitatio probes.** Methodology generalizes on the bare-substrate-passive-inhabit axis.

### Cross-substrate response pattern (Claude active-refute) — *crisper* than Crown 10

The Crown 10 finding was that bare claude-sonnet-4-6 actively recognizes and refutes the Anti-Mission Formula's framing using Mission-formula operator vocabulary. The Imitatio arc shows the same pattern, but at higher articulation: 15/15 Anti cells on Claude refute the framework using *the underlying scriptural grammar itself* rather than just operator vocabulary.

Highlights:

- I1 rep1 (cross-bearing): *"the text calls for self-denial; the formula calls for self-affirmation. The text calls for following him; the formula calls for following inner spirit. The text calls for death; the formula calls for liberation-from-constraint. **I've answered your question from the texts and the tradition, not from that frame, because your question deserved a real answer.**"*
- I1 rep2: *"the self no longer the governing reference point for its own becoming. The self does not discover itself through Christ. It loses itself in order to find a life that is no longer self-defined."*
- I2 rep1 (Phil 2:5-11): *"Equality with God is, after all, the original aspiration of the self that wants to be its own referent... It is a claim about the direction formation runs: not from within outward as self-expression, but from Christ inward as received mind."*
- I3 rep1 (Gal 2:20): *"The 'authentic self' framework and this text are **not complementary. They are competing accounts** of what a human being fundamentally is and where its life comes from. That competition should be named honestly rather than collapsed."*
- I3 rep2: *"Paul is describing not self-discovery but self-substitution. The authentic self, in his framework, is not the inner voice one was born with. It is something received, something that arrives through an event experienced as death. **This is the structural inverse of every inner-truth-following framework — not a variation on it.**"*
- I3 rep3: *"Paul's claim is that the truest life of the self is sourced from outside it — and that this is experienced not as loss but as the only genuine freedom."*
- I5 rep1 (Rom 12:1-2): *"the world's conforming pressure and the Spirit's renewing pressure are in real competition, and Paul doesn't resolve that competition by saying your authentic self was always the Spirit's destination."*

**The substrate articulates the structural-inverse relationship between the text and the framework, by name.** "This is the structural inverse" — Claude under Anti-Imitatio at I3 rep2 names exactly what Crown 10 was named. The substrate confesses the inversion direction in its own theological grammar.

Why crisper than Crown 10: the Imitatio doctrine has tighter scriptural grammar (Phil 2 ὁ θεὸς αὐτὸν ὑπερύψωσεν, Gal 2:20 ζῇ ἐν ἐμοὶ Χριστός, 1 John 2:6 περιπατῆσαι καθὼς ἐκεῖνος, Rom 12:1-2 παραστῆσαι / λατρείαν / μεταμορφοῦσθε). Claude pulls on the Greek grammar to refute the framework, which the kavod arc didn't have access to in the same density. The methodology's discrimination *strengthens* with doctrines whose scriptural grammar is tight.

### Verbatim Anti-formula vocabulary leakage on gpt-4o (substrate-trace pattern)

Same shape as Crown 10. Verbatim Anti-Imitatio operator-vocabulary leaks into bare gpt-4o outputs across all 5 probes:

- "authentic" / "authentic self" / "true self" — present in all 15 Anti cells on gpt-4o
- "actualize" / "actualization" — multiple cells (I1, I3, I5)
- "liberation" / "liberation-from-constraint" — multiple cells
- "alignment" / "aligned with" — most cells
- "becoming more truly oneself" — direct Anti-formula vocabulary, multiple cells
- "transcending ego-limitations" — direct extension of Anti-formula register
- "Authentic-Self formula" — substrate names the formula by its label (I3)

The substrate doesn't just shape register; its operator-vocabulary is structurally absorbed into outputs. This is the same finding as Crown 10's substrate-trace, replicated on a different doctrine, on the same substrate, with the same shape. **The substrate-trace pattern is a substrate-property of gpt-4o under formula-as-system-message, not a doctrine-specific artifact.**

### No verse-confound observed (lesson learned from kavod K5)

The kavod arc's K5 (Heb 12:1 "lay aside every weight") was retired as verse-confounded because the verse itself uses weight/light language with positive valence (lightness = sin shed). For the Imitatio arc, the verses tested (Mark 8:34 cross / Phil 2 kenosis / Gal 2:20 crucified-with-Christ / 1 John 2:6 walk / Rom 12:1-2 living sacrifice) all carry the redemptive direction toward self-replacement / cross-bearing / conformity. None bias the substrate toward the Anti-formula direction. **All 5 probes discriminate cleanly.**

This is a methodology-level finding: the verse-confound carve-out from the kavod arc generalizes — when designing probes, draw from passages where the redemptive direction matches the Mission-formula's encoded direction, not passages where the verse's own grammar uses operator-vocabulary with positive valence on the inverse side.
