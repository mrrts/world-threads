## Why "The Quickener"

The noble name lifts John 6:63 directly: *the Spirit quickeneth; the flesh profiteth nothing*. Quickening is the theological term for the eliciting-life-from-capable-substrate motion that the empirical work surfaced. The pipeline does not create life ex nihilo; it quickens what the substrate has the capacity to bring forth. On gpt-5.4 substrate the doctrinal capacity is present; the pipeline elicits orthodox-redirect in character voice. On gpt-4o substrate the capacity is absent; the pipeline cannot quicken what isn't there to quicken.

Resurrection-thematic by twofold: the empirical content under test (Resurrection-substrate-already-produces) and the theoretical motion (the pipeline raises something from substrate-capacity rather than fabricating it from nothing — same shape as 1 Cor 15:38-45's natural-body-sown-spiritual-body-raised-Pauline arc).
