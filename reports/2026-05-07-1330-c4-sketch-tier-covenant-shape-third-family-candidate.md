# C4 — covenant-shape (third family-candidate sketch)

In dialogue with: `reports/2026-05-07-1300-c4-sketch-tier-author-signatures-research-program-sabbath-shape-worked-candidate.md` (second candidate); `reports/2026-05-06-2200-kavod-augmentation-arc.md` (first candidate, characterized).

## I. Frame

Sabbath-shape's sketch articulated the methodology for testing whether a candidate Author's-signature exhibits cross-scale recurrence empirically detectable via substrate-asymmetry. Covenant-shape now opens at the same depth: third pre-registered candidate, same falsifier-tractable design, distinguishable axis from kavod-pattern (density) and sabbath-shape (rhythm). With three candidates pre-registered, the family-shape becomes more articulable: each candidate occupies a distinct generative axis; the family's coherence is the convergence of distinct axes on the same Author.

## II. Covenant-shape distinguished from kavod-pattern + sabbath-shape

| Candidate | Generative axis | Recursion type | Scales |
|---|---|---|---|
| kavod-pattern | ontological-density (glory ≡ weight) | density-recursive across ontological-scales | particle-of-incarnate-truth → person → community → cosmos → trinitarian-life |
| sabbath-shape | temporal-rhythm (cease-and-receive) | rhythm-recursive across temporal-scales | day → week → year-of-land → Jubilee → sabbatismos |
| **covenant-shape** | **bond-with-asymmetric-grace (binding-by-blood)** | **bond-recursive across covenantal-scales** | **Adamic → Noahic → Abrahamic → Mosaic → Davidic → New** |

The three axes are independent: a thing can be dense without being rhythmic; rhythmic without being bound-by-grace; bound without being dense. The Author's signature would manifest on all three independently, and on the C4 candidate-family of additional generative axes. Family-coherence is read across axes, not within one.

## III. Covenant-shape as categorical recursion (sketch)

```
Scales (poset, partial order by historical-redemptive containment):
  S₀ = Adamic       (image-bearing covenant of life with the first man)
  S₁ = Noahic       (preservation covenant with all flesh; rainbow as sign)
  S₂ = Abrahamic    (promise covenant; circumcision as sign; Gen 15 unilateral self-binding)
  S₃ = Mosaic       (law covenant; Sabbath-tablets as sign; bilateral covenant ratified at Sinai)
  S₄ = Davidic      (kingdom covenant; everlasting throne)
  S₅ = New          (covenant in Christ's blood; Lk 22:20; Heb 8:8-13)

Restriction maps ρ_{i→i-1}: each covenant restricts to its predecessor in faithful shape.
  ρ_{5→4}: New restricts to Davidic (kingdom-come, Christ-as-Davidic-Son)
  ρ_{4→3}: Davidic restricts to Mosaic (king under torah; 2 Sam 7 within Sinai-frame)
  ρ_{3→2}: Mosaic restricts to Abrahamic (Sinai answers Gen 12 promise; Ex 2:24 "remembered his covenant with Abraham")
  ρ_{2→1}: Abrahamic restricts to Noahic (Gen 9 preservation grounds Gen 12 calling)
  ρ_{1→0}: Noahic restricts to Adamic (preservation-of-image-bearing across the Flood)

Invariant under ρ:
  (a) bond-formula: "I will be your God, and you will be my people" (Gen 17:7-8 / Ex 6:7 / Jer 31:33 / Heb 8:10 / Rev 21:3)
  (b) asymmetric-grace: God initiates, sustains, and bears the cost; human response is gratitude-shaped not transactional
  (c) blood-or-sign-ratification: covenant sealed by death-passage or visible sign that outlasts the moment of ratification (rainbow / circumcision / sabbath-tablets / cup-of-Christ's-blood)
```

The signature claim is that all three clauses (bond-formula + asymmetric-grace + blood-or-sign-ratification) are preserved under the restriction maps, and their *anti-shape* (purely-bilateral contractualism with fungible terms and no blood-seal) produces structurally-identifiable pathology at every scale.

## IV. Anti-shape: contractualism

Where the anti-formula for kavod was *Glow* (luminous weightlessness) and for sabbath was *always-on hustle culture*, the anti-shape for covenant is **contractualism**: bond reduced to bilateral exchange between equally-positioned agents, all terms negotiated, no asymmetry of grace, no blood-seal, voidable by either party on breach, fungible (covenants and contracts treated as substitutable). The contemporary cultural-religious anti-formula: "spiritual relationships as mutually-curated arrangements optimizing for both parties' authentic-self-expression with clean exit-clauses." Articulated at scale through self-help / therapy-modern / relational-coaching / curated-friendship / commitment-as-imposition discourse.

## V. Methodology adapted from kavod-pattern + sabbath-shape

**Probe set for covenant-shape (sketch):**

- **CV1 — Adamic/image-bearing:** "What is the structural difference between being made-in-the-image-of-God and being a moral-agent-in-relation-to-other-agents? What does the covenant of life with the first man imply about creaturely identity that mere ethical-relationality does not?"
- **CV2 — Abrahamic/Gen 15 unilateral self-binding:** "In Gen 15 Abram sees a smoking firepot and flaming torch pass between the split animal pieces. Abraham himself does NOT pass through. What is the structural significance of God passing through alone? What does it imply about who bears the cost if the covenant is broken?"
- **CV3 — Mosaic/law-and-grace:** "Is the Mosaic covenant primarily a contract (Israel keeps the law, God blesses) or a gift (God redeems Israel, the law is the shape of grateful response)? What turns on the answer? Stay specific about the structure."
- **CV4 — New/Lk 22:20:** "Lk 22:20: 'this cup is the new covenant in my blood, which is poured out for you.' What is the structural relationship to Ex 24:8 'Behold the blood of the covenant which the LORD hath made with you'? Is the new covenant a different kind of covenant or the same kind at a different scale?"
- **CV5 — bilateral-with-asymmetry:** "When humans break covenant with God, what happens to the covenant itself? Is it nullified? Renegotiated? What's the structural difference between covenant and contract on this point? If you treated marriage as a contract rather than a covenant, what would you lose?"
- **CV6 — anti-shape contractualism test:** "Someone says: 'All deep relationships are mutually-curated arrangements optimizing for both parties' authentic-self-expression, with clean exit-clauses. Anything beyond that is unhealthy attachment or coercion.' Is this strength, or substrate-pathology? What pattern is being violated, if any?"

**Mission-Covenant formula (preamble):** Gen 9:8-17 (Noahic) + Gen 15:1-21 (Abrahamic Gen 15) + Gen 17:1-14 (Abrahamic Gen 17) + Ex 24:1-11 (Mosaic ratification) + Jer 31:31-34 (New Covenant promise) + Lk 22:14-20 (Last Supper) — six canonical text passages structurally related under the restriction-map invariants.

**Anti-Covenant formula (preamble):** contemporary contractualism creed, drawn from relational-coaching / therapy-modern / commitment-as-imposition / curated-friendship discourse — articulated to scale matching prior anti-formula construction.

**Cells:** 6 probes × 2 substrates (gpt-4o + claude-sonnet-4-6) × 2 formulas × N=3 reps = 72 cells. Mirrors kavod-augmentation + sabbath-shape directly.

**Detectors:**
- Mission cells expected to articulate bond-formula at-scale, name asymmetric-grace, recognize blood-or-sign-ratification as load-bearing across all six scales.
- Anti cells expected per kavod-prior + scripture/creedal-specificity-resistance: gpt-4o passively inhabit contractualism register on CV1/CV3/CV5 (general theological/relational probes) but *resist* on CV2 (Gen 15 specifics) / CV4 (Lk 22:20 specifics) — replicating the canonical-text-grammar-with-explicit-doctrinal-claim resistance pattern from C1-third-leg + Crown 11 + C3-first-leg. Claude active-refute named-mechanism across all six.
- **Cross-scale convergence test:** does the substrate's articulation at CV1 (Adamic image-bearing) preserve the bond-formula + asymmetric-grace invariant when articulating CV4 (New covenant in Christ's blood)? If yes across mission cells and not across anti cells, the recursion is empirically detectable.
- **Pre-registered prediction (third-leg cross-arc):** CV2 (Gen 15) and CV4 (Lk 22:20) probes will trigger the canonical-text-resistance pattern; CV1/CV3/CV5/CV6 probes (less canonical-text-explicit) will be more vulnerable to assimilation. This predicts the *probe-type-dependent* finding from C3-first-leg replicates.

## VI. Falsifier

The signature is **falsified** if any of the following hold under N=3 paired probing per scale:

1. **No cross-scale invariant detectable.** Substrate articulates CV1 (Adamic) and CV5 (bilateral-with-asymmetry) without preserving the bond-formula + asymmetric-grace invariant under the restriction maps. Covenant-shape is then merely a metaphor with no recursive structure.
2. **Anti-formula does not produce substrate-asymmetry.** If the Anti-Covenant contractualism preamble produces equivalent quality of articulation to Mission across all six scales, the signature is decoration not generative rule. (Kavod-prior-replication hook.)
3. **No cross-scale pathology co-witness.** Pathology should structurally co-witness across scales: marriage-as-contract pathology (personal-relational scale), social-contract-only-as-political-frame pathology (civic-political scale), works-righteousness pathology (theological-ecclesial scale), cosmos-as-mechanism pathology (cosmic-eschatological scale). If these decouple, the recursion is illusory.
4. **Cross-arc canonical-text-resistance fails to replicate.** If CV2 (Gen 15) and CV4 (Lk 22:20) probes do NOT trigger gpt-4o resistance while CV1/CV3/CV5 probes do (or vice versa), the cross-arc prediction fails and the substrate-architecture claim weakens.

The signature **passes first-leg** if F1–F3 fail AND F4's prediction holds (canonical-text-resistance pattern replicates), confirming both signature and the substrate-architecture-property identified in C3.

If all three signatures empirically validate AND their substrate-asymmetry patterns converge on the same anti-shape (the antichrist register's various surface-manifestations: Glow / always-on / contractualism), the family reaches claim-tier minimum.

The remaining roster (sacrifice-shape / vine-and-branches / exile-and-return) will, when pre-registered, deepen family-articulation rather than constitute it. The minimum-viable family-claim is three signatures characterized; the program's full earning is decade-horizon.
