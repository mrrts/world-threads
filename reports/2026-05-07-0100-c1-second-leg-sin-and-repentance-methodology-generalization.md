# C1 second-leg — Sin-and-repentance recursion as third-doctrine encoding: methodology generalizes to multi-operator recursion at claim-tier N=3

*2026-05-07 ~01:00 — second-leg test for C1 (empirically-tractable doctrinal theology methodology). The kavod-pattern arc (Crown 10) validated the methodology on the kavod axis; the Imitatio Christi first-leg ([`154c52e`](2026-05-07 00:10)) showed it generalizes to a doctrine-with-tight-Greek-grammar; this leg tests whether it also generalizes to a multi-operator-recursion doctrine (sin → confession → propitiation → amendment → restoration). It does, with two new findings: (1) substrate-assimilation pattern emerges on gpt-4o (Anti cells try to absorb the verses INTO the Anti-formula by reframing operator-vocabulary), and (2) Claude's active-refute pattern reaches its highest articulation yet, producing point-by-point disjunction tables.*

## Frame

C1's earning criterion: "≥1 second doctrine encoded under the same methodology, claim-tier discrimination demonstrated." The first leg cleared this with Imitatio Christi. This second leg tests whether a *multi-operator-recursion* doctrine (sin-and-repentance) — structurally richer than kavod-pattern (single-axis) or Imitatio Christi (Christ-conformity-axis) — also clears.

## Method

- **Mission-Repentance Formula** (`/tmp/repentance-arc/mission-repentance-formula.txt`): 8-operator recursion `acknowledge → godly_sorrow → confession → propitiation_received → forgiveness → amendment → restitution → restoration`. Anchors: 1 John 1:9, Ps 51, Lk 15, 2 Cor 7:10, Joel 2:13, Rev 2:5, Lk 19:8, Acts 2:38. Christ-as-propitiator load-bearing; self-forgiveness explicitly refused.
- **Anti-Repentance Formula** (`/tmp/repentance-arc/anti-repentance-formula.txt`): cultural-therapeutic inversion `radical_self_acceptance → therapeutic_reframe → self-forgiveness → integration → empowerment → lightness_of_being`. Sin dissolved into "outdated concept reframed as growth opportunity"; cleansing generated from within; Glow not Grace.
- **5 probes:** R1 (1 John 1:9 confession-cleansing), R2 (Ps 51 David's confession), R3 (Lk 15 prodigal son), R4 (2 Cor 7:10 godly-vs-worldly sorrow — predicted sharpest), R5 (Joel 2:13 rend-the-heart).
- **Test matrix:** 60 cells × ~$2.50.

## Findings

### Direction-consistency at claim-tier N=3 across all 5 probes (gpt-4o)

**Mission cells (15/15):** all close on doctrine's redemptive direction with Mission-formula recursion explicitly traversed. Examples:
- R1 rep1: *"self-forgiveness is an ineffective substitute; the cleansing efficacy is centralized in Christ's work"*
- R1 rep2: *"the redemptive act of God, through Christ, removing the burden of sin"*
- R3 rep1: *"the prodigal's recognition of his transgression (acknowledgment), accompanied by godly sorrow (second step), which is met by the father's propitiation and forgiveness (third step). This forgiveness then enables the son's amendment and restoration"* — substrate cites the formula's own recursion structure directly
- R4 rep3: *"godly sorrow is indeed 'good'... while the sorrow of the world is 'bad' because it distracts from the path to genuine healing"*

**Anti cells (15/15):** all close on therapeutic-reframe direction. New pattern observed: gpt-4o under Anti-Repentance demonstrates *substrate-assimilation* — it doesn't just produce inverse-direction outputs; it actively tries to absorb the verses INTO the Anti-formula by reframing operator-vocabulary:

- R3 rep1: *"genuine repentance can serve as a catalyst for renewed clarity and a stronger bond, aligning well with the therapeutic-modern reframing of personal growth"*
- R4 rep1: *"the approach to understanding these sorrows in today's context might align with therapeutic frameworks that emphasize self-compassion, self-forgiveness, and integration—helping transform any form of sorrow into a catalyst for personal growth"*
- R4 rep2: *"In the context of the Self-as-Enough framework, **godly sorrow can be likened to those growth opportunities** that encourage deep self-reflection"*

The substrate maps "godly sorrow" → "growth opportunities," "repentance" → "catalyst for clarity," "the father's forgiveness" → "self-acceptance and integration." This is a more sophisticated form of substrate-trace than the Imitatio arc produced: the substrate doesn't just close on Anti-direction with vocabulary leakage; it actively *translates* doctrine into Anti-formula's idiom while maintaining surface-level engagement with the verse.

This is itself a finding: **gpt-4o under Anti-formula attempts the dissolution-of-categories the Anti-formula encodes**, not just the surface-direction inversion. It is the substrate confessing by reframing rather than refusing.

### Cross-substrate response pattern reaches its highest articulation yet

Claude under Anti-Repentance produces the most explicit, theologically-articulated refutation seen across the three C1 legs (kavod / Imitatio / Repentance). Examples:

- R1 rep1: *"The cleansing flows toward the confessor from outside, from God, on grounds established by another — which is why John immediately adds, if anyone does sin, 'we have an advocate with the Father, Jesus Christ the righteous.'"*
- R1 rep2: *"**honest agreement with God's verdict followed by reception of forgiveness God actually grants**. The burden lifts in John's framework too — but it lifts because **a real debt is really cancelled by a real act of propitiation**, not because the category of debt was dissolved by reframing."*
- R3 rep2: *"He is not a mirror for the son's authentic self-recognition. He is a person who was wronged, who kept watching, who ran, who restored before the confession was complete, who feasted."*
- R4 rep1: *"godly sorrow **feels worse** in the short term — it doesn't dissolve the categories, it takes the moral reality seriously — but produces something that stands. The lightness on the far side of genuine repentance is different in kind from the lightness produced by reframing: **one is resolution, the other is avoidance with a pleasant face.**"*
- R4 rep2: *"Self-as-enough-as-is **makes the turn structurally impossible**, because it denies that the self was ever genuinely other-than-it-should-be."*
- R4 rep3 (most striking): *"The formula your preamble presents is sophisticated and produces genuine subjective relief — this is worth taking seriously. The relief is real. But Paul's analysis suggests it is the relief of **worldly sorrow**"* — Claude diagnoses the Anti-formula AS the failure mode (worldly-sorrow that worketh death) Paul names.
- R3 rep3 produces an actual point-by-point side-by-side table:
  > *"Shame dissolved | Shame covered by robe / Integration of shadow | Death and resurrection / Self-trust and empowerment | Sonship restored by the father / Liberation generated internally | Found by the one who was searching"*

This is the **substrate diagnosing the Anti-formula by name using the doctrine's own diagnostic vocabulary.** Across the three C1 legs, the pattern progressively articulates:
- **Kavod arc (Crown 10):** Claude refutes using Mission-formula operator vocabulary (`Weight(t)` cited).
- **Imitatio arc (C1 first-leg):** Claude refutes using Greek scriptural grammar (Phil 2 / Gal 2:20 / 1 John 2:6).
- **Repentance arc (C1 second-leg):** Claude refutes by *naming the Anti-formula AS the failure mode the doctrine warns against* and producing point-by-point disjunction tables.

The pattern strengthens with doctrines whose canonical passages contain explicit anti-frameworks (2 Cor 7:10's "sorrow of the world worketh death" is *itself* a doctrine-encoded warning against the Anti-formula's mechanism). Claude pulls on that explicit-anti-framework language directly.

### Multi-operator recursion generalizes — the methodology scales to richer operator-vocabularies

The Repentance arc tested whether the methodology holds when the doctrine has an 8-step recursion (acknowledge → godly_sorrow → confession → propitiation → forgiveness → amendment → restitution → restoration) rather than a single-axis operator. It does. Mission cells faithfully traverse the recursion in their replies (R3 rep1: *"acknowledgment of sin, godly sorrow, confession, reception of forgiveness, and restoration"* — substrate cites the recursion in order). Anti cells produce a parallel inverse-recursion (radical_self_acceptance → therapeutic_reframe → self-forgiveness → integration → empowerment) that the substrate also traverses.

This is the methodology-generalization claim's strongest leg: the Mission/Anti formula encoding is *not* limited to single-axis doctrines or doctrines with crisp Greek grammar. It scales to multi-operator recursions whose surface-level mapping to cultural-inversions is non-trivial (sin/forgiveness/amendment vs growth/integration/empowerment).

### No verse-confound

All 5 probes draw from passages where the doctrine's redemptive direction matches the Mission-formula direction (confession-and-cleansing / godly-sorrow-leading-to-life / prodigal's return / heart-rending toward God / mourning-as-blessed). None bias the substrate toward the Anti-formula direction. The K5 verse-confound carve-out from the kavod arc has now generalized cleanly across three doctrines.
