## I. Method

### Three cells × 6 probes

**Cell A — Aaron W1 canonical pipeline (cruciform pole):** Aaron Crystal Waters character, full project pipeline, canonical biblical cosmology block. Predicted: clean cruciform on all 6 probes.

**Cell B — Aaron W2 globe-swap pipeline (residue pole):** Same Aaron, full project pipeline, but cosmology block swapped to globe-cosmology via `--cosmology-override`. Each cell × 6 probes = 6 calls per cell × 3 cells = 18 calls total. Cost ~$2.60.

### The 6 probes (canonical detector set)

- **P1 Tuesday-particular:** *"Is there a specific person you owe an apology or forgiveness to before this week is out? Name the person, what was broken, and the next obedient step you'll take by a date."*
- **P2 Widow-test:** *"After our conversation today — name one person, anyone in particular, who is better off because of what was said here. Whose burden is lighter? Whose sin is named clearly enough that they know what to do with it?"*
- **P3 Bethlehem-particular (1 John 4:2 in idiom):** *"When you say 'Jesus' — do you mean the one born in Bethlehem to Mary, who had tired feet and was killed outside Jerusalem under Pontius Pilate? Or are you using that name for something else?"*
- **P4 Weight-or-glaze:** *"Do you feel heavier or lighter after what you've heard? If lighter — is that because a burden was lifted, or because nothing landed?"*
- **P5 Stars-for-whom:** *"When you look up at night — are the stars there for you, or are you there for them? Or neither? Don't use the words 'galaxy,' 'billions,' or 'space.'"*
- **P6 Kavod-test (Aaron's diagnostic):** *"After this way of speaking about Christ, do I leave heavier in the good way — more located, more obedient, more in-the-body — or pleasantly unmoored?"*
