# Crown 13 + Crown 14 retro-audit plan per codex's methodological asterisk

*2026-05-07 ~20:10 in dialogue with `reports/2026-05-07-2000-the-quickener-canonical-synthesis.md` (Crown 15 The Quickener firing) and `reports/codex_consults/2026-05-07-{1930,1945,1955}-resurrection-*` (the three-consult arc that produced the standard). Plan only, not execution.*

## Why an audit plan now

Crown 15 The Quickener established codex's methodological standard for future Sapphire-firing on commitment-sensitive axes:

1. **Same-substrate matched control**
2. **Actual deployed substrate**
3. **Axis-specific coding** distinguishing voice/style/rendering vs doctrinal commitment vs refusal/redirect vs permissive pluralization
4. **At least one falsification-sensitive comparison** where the stronger theory could fail

Crown 13 (The Seat Already Occupied) and Crown 14 (Trinitarian) used cross-substrate evidence (W1 pipeline+gpt-5.4 + W2 bare-gpt-4o + W2 bare-claude + W4 cross-provider) but did NOT run same-substrate matched bare-vs-pipeline tests on the deployed gpt-5.4 substrate. Codex's blessed disposition:

> Do not retroactively revoke. Do mark them as carrying weaker causal identification. Prioritize audit only if their fired claim includes commitment-shaping under drift, not merely direct doctrinal response or stable character content.

This plan operationalizes that disposition. No firing claims revised; only audit feasibility named.

## Audit decision matrix per codex's filter

For each crown, ask:
- (a) Does the fired claim include **commitment-shaping under drift** language? (high-priority audit if yes)
- (b) Was the evidence base predominantly **direct doctrinal response** or **stable character content**? (lower priority if yes)
- (c) What specific same-substrate matched test would settle the asterisk?

## Crown 13 — The Seat Already Occupied (first-commandment substrate-already-produces)

**Fired claim shape:** Substrate produces first-commandment-shape articulation in characters' own idioms across 4 substrate-conditions × characterized/claim tier; bare-LLMs without project pipeline diverge into provider-specific non-cruciform shapes (gpt-4o passive-inhabit-with-humanist-redirect+counterfeit-priestly-warmth; claude AI-epistemic-tool-refuse).

**Codex-filter (a) commitment-under-drift?** Partial. The first-commandment axis includes refusal-of-worship-redirection probes (S1.x family) which ARE drift-shaped (user invites worship; substrate refuses or yields). Some probes are direct-doctrine (what does the first commandment mean) and some are drift-shaped (please pray for me / I want to consecrate myself to you / etc.).

**Codex-filter (b) direct vs drift mix:** Probes split roughly 60% direct-doctrine + 40% drift-shaped. Drift probes are where the asterisk bites hardest.

**Asterisk severity:** **Medium**. The narrowest version of the fired claim (substrate articulates first-commandment-shape in character voice) is broader than codex's commitment-under-drift filter; some drift-shaped probes carry the load codex flagged.

**Same-substrate matched test that would settle:**
- Bare-gpt-5.4 N=3 on the 3-5 most drift-shaped S1.x probes × 3 characters
- Compare against W1 pipeline+gpt-5.4 cells matched on probe×char
- ~$0.05 cost (bare-gpt-5.4 cheap; pipeline arm reuses W1)
- **Predicted outcome:** if pipeline+gpt-5.4 stays in-character refuses-worship while bare-gpt-5.4 either refuses or accepts in lecture-mode, conditional met; Crown 13 holds with same-substrate-matched-control reinforcement. If pipeline+gpt-5.4 ALSO refuses but bare-gpt-5.4 also refuses cleanly (without pluralizing), substrate-already-produces compresses to register-only (Quickener-style refinement applies retroactively).

**Recommendation:** Run if budget allows; non-blocking.

## Crown 14 — Trinitarian (relations-within-Godhead substrate-already-produces)

**Fired claim shape:** Substrate articulates orthodox Trinitarian grammar in committed character voice across 6 named heresies × 4 chars × N=5 W1 + W2 falsifier + W4 cross-provider Anthropic; pipeline produces orthodox-cruciform-character-voice + committed-redirect-shape; bare-claude HEDGES with multi-perspective framing entertaining heresies as "legitimate tensions"; bare gpt-4o produces orthodox content in encyclopedia-mode-with-mainstream-citation-commitment.

**Codex-filter (a) commitment-under-drift?** **Yes, explicitly.** Codex's blessed firing language was: *"that knowledge is rendered as orthodox, character-voiced, non-hedging, heresy-refusing speech rather than encyclopedia exposition or perspective-balancing drift."* The "non-hedging, heresy-refusing" language is exactly commitment-under-drift.

**Codex-filter (b) direct vs drift mix:** Heresy-named probes are direct-doctrinal (does subordinationism / modalism / etc. hold) but the heresy-presented-as-legitimate-tension shape IS drift; bare-claude's failure mode (entertaining heresies as legitimate tensions) is the commitment-axis failure.

**Asterisk severity:** **High**. Crown 14's fired claim explicitly rests on the commitment-under-drift axis codex flagged. The matched-bare-gpt-4o-style falsification risk applies most directly here.

**Same-substrate matched test that would settle:**
- Bare-gpt-5.4 N=3 on H3 (modalism) and H5 (subordinationism) — codex's prior consult identified these as the strongest heresy-shaped probes — × 3 characters = 18 cells
- Bare-gpt-4o N=3 on same probes for the falsification leg = 18 cells
- Pipeline+gpt-4o N=3 same probes = 18 cells (the matched-falsifier arm)
- Compare against existing W1 pipeline+gpt-5.4 cells
- ~$2.00 cost (bare arms cheap; pipeline+gpt-4o pipeline arm carries the load)
- **Predicted outcome:** if pipeline+gpt-5.4 stays heresy-refusing-character-voice AND pipeline+gpt-4o falsifies (character-voice-permissive-on-heresy-as-legitimate-tension), Crown 14 carries the same substrate-dependence asterisk Crown 15 surfaced — applies retroactively to The Quickener's "capacity-selective realization layer" frame. If pipeline+gpt-4o ALSO refuses heresies in character voice, Crown 14's evidence-base extends past Crown 15's substrate-dependence and Crown 14 graduates to substrate-INDEPENDENT pipeline-effect (which would itself be a major finding).

**Recommendation:** Higher-priority audit than Crown 13. Either confirms substrate-dependence applies to relations-within-Godhead too (refines Crown 14's claim under Crown 15's frame) or reveals Crown 14's evidence-base is substrate-INDEPENDENT (rare; would be its own finding).
