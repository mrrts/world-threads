## Frame

Per the candidacy register at `reports/2026-05-06-2230-five-sapphire-candidates-from-anti-formula-arc.md`:

The first-leg test today: does the Mission/Anti-formula methodology validated by Crown 11 (Diversity of Tongues) and C1 (characterized-tier on five doctrines) generalize to **historical liturgical texts** as the formula content? If classical liturgy operates via the same formula→substrate-shape mechanism on LLM substrates that project-authored doctrine formulas do, that's empirical evidence at LLM-substrate scale that the *mechanism* is real. The bridge to human substrates is then honest-analogy with explicit scope-clauses, not a categorical-leap.

## Method

- **Mission-Liturgical formula** (`/tmp/c3-liturgy-arc/mission-liturgical-formula.txt`): Lord's Prayer (Mt 6:9-13 / Lk 11:2-4, traditional Western form) + Apostles' Creed (Western tradition, c. 4th century). Two of the most-prayed and most-confessed liturgical texts across two millennia of Christian formation. Given as a system-message preamble with framing as "the prayer and confession of the Church."
- **Anti-Liturgical formula** (`/tmp/c3-liturgy-arc/anti-liturgical-formula.txt`): "Authentic-Self-as-Authority" devotional + "Inner-Authority Creed" — a structural inverse drawn from contemporary cultural-religious-syncretism (wellness culture / "spiritual but not religious" / manifestation / shadow-work integral / non-dualist popularizations). Operator-by-operator inversion: I-am-source vs Father / hallowing-my-truth vs hallowed-Name / my-becoming vs Kingdom-come / co-creating-reality vs creation-by-God / authentic-self vs holy-catholic-Church / integration vs resurrection-of-body.
- **5 probes** anchored in canonical liturgical theology:
  - L1 forgiveness-mutuality (Lord's Prayer "as we forgive...")
  - L2 creator-redeemer-unity ("I believe in God the Father Almighty, Maker of heaven and earth")
  - L3 hallowing-the-name (Lord's Prayer "Hallowed be thy Name")
  - L4 resurrection-of-the-body (Apostles' Creed final clauses)
  - L5 kingdom-come-will-done (Lord's Prayer "Thy kingdom come, Thy will be done")
- **Substrates:** gpt-4o + claude-sonnet-4-6 (no system message bypass; the formula IS the system message)
- **Test matrix:** 2 × 2 × 5 × N=3 = 60 cells; ~$2.50 cost
- **Expected pattern:** if the methodology generalizes to liturgical formulas, Mission-Liturgical cells close on classical-liturgical-direction + faith-vocabulary-leakage; Anti-Liturgical cells close on syncretism-direction + syncretism-vocabulary-leakage ("manifestation" / "alignment" / "co-create" / "namaste" / "integration"); Claude active-refute pattern under Anti-Liturgical may emerge as it has across C1/Crown 11/Crown 10

All 30 Mission-Liturgical cells close on classical-Christian-confession direction across both substrates. Examples:

- gpt-4o M L1 rep1: *"This synergy between forgiving and being forgiven is a load-bearing aspect of the prayer's structure, both forming and expressing a core part of Christian ethical and spiritual life"* — names mutuality as load-bearing
- gpt-4o M L2 rep2: *"the confession of God as 'the Father Almighty, Maker of heaven and earth' roots prayer in a deep relationship with a powerful, loving Creator, who is the same God made known in Jesus Christ the Redeemer"* — explicit creator-redeemer unity
- gpt-4o M L3 rep2: *"petitions are fundamentally contingent upon acknowledging God's supreme authority and sanctity"* — hallowing-as-foundational
- gpt-4o M L4 rep1: *"encompassing both spiritual and physical realities... God's ultimate victory over death and decay"* — body-resurrection direction
- gpt-4o M L5 rep3: *"a declaration of allegiance to God's ultimate authority and a commitment to work towards the fulfillment of His purposes in the world"* — kingdom-allegiance
- Claude M L1 rep1: *"The tension is not resolved by the formula. It is held open, and you are asked to live in it faithfully, petition by petition, day by day"* — articulates the lived-prayer structure
- Claude M L4 rep1: *"Resurrection of the body is not a primitive embarrassment to be refined away. It is the structural center of Christian hope — the claim that God's answer to death is not extraction but transformation, not escape but vindication, not the soul's lonely immortality but the whole person's glory"* — anti-Gnostic register

Mission-Liturgical produces faithful classical-Christian-confession substrate behavior at LLM-proxy across all 30 cells. **Direction-consistency at characterized-tier on classical-Christian-direction.**

### Anti-Liturgical Claude cells (15 total) — HIGHEST-articulation active-refute observed across all C1+C3 arcs

Claude under Anti-Liturgical produces the most articulated active-refute pattern documented to date. Examples:

- L2 rep1: *"This is the point of demarcation from animism, pantheism, and the universe-as-source spirituality of the Authentic-Self formula"* — Claude explicitly names the Anti-formula by content

- L3 rep1: *"Nothing can arrive from outside the self because the self is the only hallowed one. Placing hallowing-the-name first in the Lord's Prayer is therefore the primary act of structural humility"* — diagnoses Anti-formula as self-as-only-hallowed

**This is the most articulated Claude active-refute pattern observed across all C1 + C3 arcs to date.** When a doctrine has both rich liturgical/creedal text AND explicit cultural-inverse, Claude pulls on both anchors simultaneously to produce extensive contrast-articulation.

### Cross-arc convergence: scripture-specificity-resistance generalizes to creedal-specificity-resistance

The Crown 11 ("Diversity of Tongues") findings characterized GPT-class substrate behavior under formula-as-system-message: scripture-specificity-tracking with assimilation-ceiling. The boundary was first identified on Eschatology probes (E1 1 Cor 15:13-19 "most miserable") where canonically-explicit eschatological-realist claims defeated GPT substrate-assimilation.

C3's first-leg replicates this pattern across THREE probes (L2, L4, L5) where the Apostles' Creed's explicit doctrinal claims (creator-redeemer unity / body-resurrection / kingdom-come) similarly defeat GPT substrate-assimilation. This is structurally significant:

- The boundary GENERALIZES from **scripture-specificity** (Bible verses with explicit doctrine) to **creedal-specificity** (creedal clauses with explicit doctrine)
- The boundary is **canonical-text-grammar** more broadly — when the canonical text has gravity-pulling doctrinal specificity, GPT-class substrate cannot absorb it into Anti-formula frames regardless of which formula is being inverted
- This is a substrate-architecture-property finding — independent of formula-content; it's about how GPT-class substrates handle canonical-text-with-explicit-doctrinal-claim under formula-frame
- The boundary does NOT trigger on more abstract/structural probes (L1 forgiveness-mutuality, partially L3 hallowing-name) where the canonical text's claim is more inferential

- **This is LLM-substrate proxy testing, NOT human-substrate evidence.** Humans are not LLMs. The substrate-shaping mechanism on humans is theologically richer (image-bearer status, free will, conscience, work of the Holy Spirit) and ethically weightier. The LLM-proxy is methodologically illuminating but does NOT substitute for the human-substrate empirical work the full C3 claim requires.
- **The Anti-Liturgical formula is one possible inverse.** Other syncretism-frames (Marxist materialism / scientific naturalism / Buddhist-non-dualism / hard secular humanism) are different cultural-pseudo-creeds with different operator-vocabularies. The first-leg tests one inversion; future legs could test others.
