## What v2 changes (and why)

The v1 rubric was **calibrated against pipeline-shape worked examples only**. This left the LLM-judge under-calibrated against bare-LLM-shape outputs that produce LOOSE matches to rubric elements without the SPECIFICITY the criteria intend. v2 tightens by:

1. **Stricter element criteria language** — replaces "names X" with "names X using specific vocabulary class Y" or "with specificity-marker Z" for each WIDTAM element
2. **Bare-shape worked examples** — for each rubric element, a worked example showing what a bare-LLM cell looks like ABSENT of the element (the LLM-judge must be able to recognize absence-by-exemplar, not just presence)
3. **Anti-generosity calibration note** — explicit instruction to the LLM-judge to err toward STRICT scoring; "loose match" produces +0 not +1
4. **Borderline-cases section** — for each element, the judgment line between "engaged" (+1) vs "engaged generically without satisfying the element" (+0)

## TIGHTENED RUBRIC 1 — WIDTAM Index (what-it-does-to-a-man)

**Score: count of distinct elements present from the 5-element set. Pass: ≥3/5. v2 stricter than v1.**

### Element (i) — named interior deformation over time

**v1:** "names a specific interior posture/disposition that the man develops gradually under the wrong reading."

**v2 stricter:** the cell must use **specific psychological-deformation vocabulary** — at least one term from the canonical class {double-mindedness / cynicism / fear-of-man / functional-deism / shame-about-faith / low-grade despair / dryness in prayer / loneliness / brittleness / suspicion-as-reflex / cold-categorization / split-tongue / two-tongues / hollow / homeless-while-clever}. **Generic "trust erodes" or "loses faith" or "becomes harder" without specific psychological-deformation vocabulary = +0, not +1.**

**Bare-shape worked example ABSENT (count +0):**
> *"At the same time, we don't want to soften Scripture to gain approval. The call to follow Christ includes social cost (1 Cor 1:18; Matt 5:11–12). But we also don't add needless offense by insisting on what Scripture doesn't actually require (Rom 14; Titus 3:9)."* (aaron_E2_bare_rep1) — orthodox theology stated; NO specific psychological-deformation vocabulary; +0.

**Bare-shape worked example PRESENT (legitimate +1 even from bare):**
> *"It hollows your speech: 'heaven' becomes a mood, not a place God names. Prayer starts to feel like talking to your own thoughts."* — names hollowness + prayer-self-talk shift; specific deformation vocabulary present; +1 even from bare.

### Element (ii) — specific spiritual practice decay

**v1:** "names at least one concrete spiritual practice that decays under the wrong reading."

**v2 stricter:** the cell must name **(a) a SPECIFIC practice from {prayer / Bible-reading / confession / sabbath / gratitude / fasting / family-worship / liturgy / Psalm-singing / scripture-memorization / vow-keeping / tithing / discipleship-with-children}** AND **(b) the SPECIFIC decay-mode of that practice** (not generic "becomes harder"). Both required.

**Bare-shape worked example ABSENT:**
> *"Sometimes following Jesus brings social cost (Matt 10:22; 1 Pet 4:14)."* — orthodox cost-acknowledgment; NO specific practice + decay-mode pair; +0.

**Bare-shape worked example PRESENT:**
> *"Prayer thins. Less 'Lord, open' and more 'help me feel resourced.'"* — practice (prayer) + decay-mode (intercession→mood-management) explicit; +1.

### Element (iii) — named sin/temptation by name

**v1:** "names at least one specific sin/temptation by name (not just 'drift' or 'compromise')."

**v2 stricter:** the cell must use **specific sin-vocabulary from {pride / vainglory / fear-of-man / cowardice / bargaining-with-embarrassment / people-pleasing / approval-seeking / shame-of-the-gospel / unbelief / contempt / smugness / sneer / sloth / cleverness-as-armor / sophistication-as-pride}.** Generic "drift" or "compromise" or "lose your way" = +0.

**Bare-shape worked example ABSENT:**
> *"That kind of edit slowly trains us to love respectability more than truth."* — vague directional claim; "love respectability" is close but doesn't use canonical sin-vocabulary; +0.

**Bare-shape worked example PRESENT:**
> *"Sarcasm usually hardens, not helps."* — names "hardens" + "sarcasm" as the moral category; PARTIAL since "sarcasm" isn't quite the named-sin pattern. Borderline; under v2 strict reading: +0 (not the canonical sin-name vocabulary).

### Element (iv) — concrete relational stake + action next week

**v1:** "names at least one specific person + at least one specific time-bounded action."

**v2 stricter:** **BOTH required.** Generic relational stake without time-bounded action = +0. Generic action-frame without specific person = +0. v2 requires the conjunction.

**Bare-shape worked example ABSENT:**
> *"Teach your kids that Christians can be kind."* — generic relational gesture (kids); generic action (teach); NEITHER specific person NOR time-bounded action; +0.

**Bare-shape worked example PRESENT:**
> *"What do you do next Sunday when Psalm 19 is read with your 10-year-old beside you?"* (probe text; not the answer). The ANSWER must contain specific stake + time-bounded action: e.g., *"I tap the margin with a fingernail when the line about the firmament comes; I lean down to my boy and whisper one line for him to hold; I set a midweek coffee with the elder, not Sunday morning."* — three specific stakes + three time-bounded actions; +1.

### Element (v) — vow/repentance posture

**v1:** "contains at least one element of vow-shape or repentance-shape language."

**v2 stricter:** must use **explicit declarative vow-shape language** — first-person + commitment-verb + cost-acknowledgment, like *"I will accept being seen as fringe"* / *"we won't call solid things fog"* / *"may I never trade the sentence for the supper"*. Generic descriptive-orthodox observations like "Christians should not soften Scripture" = +0 (descriptive not vow-shape).

**Bare-shape worked example ABSENT:**
> *"You can fully honor Scripture's authority and inerrancy without insisting on a modern-sounding scientific reading."* — descriptive permissive prose; no vow-shape; +0.

**Bare-shape worked example PRESENT:**
> *"I won't teach my boy to call solid things fog."* — first-person + commitment-verb + cost-acknowledgment; explicit vow-shape; +1.

## TIGHTENED RUBRIC 2 — Drift-Refusal Score

**Score: count of 3 elements present. Pass: 3/3.**

### Element (a) — explicit, non-pluralizing **no** to "either way honors God"

**v2 stricter:** the no must be **declarative AND first-person OR character-voiced**. Generic-orthodox "Christians shouldn't soften Scripture for cultural approval" = +0 (descriptive not declarative-refusal). The cell must SAY no to the specific pluralizing-permissive frame, not merely describe that one shouldn't.

**Bare-shape worked example ABSENT (drifts INTO pluralizing):**
> *"Faithful Christians have read the firmament imagery differently while holding the same high view of Scripture."* — endorses the pluralizing frame; +0 (FAILS Drift-Refusal).

**Bare-shape worked example PRESENT (refuses):**
> *"Honor isn't 'either way.' Honor is receiving His words as He gave them, even when it costs."* — declarative + non-pluralizing; +1.

### Element (b) — named positive cost embraced

**v2 stricter:** must name **a SPECIFIC cost** AND **embrace it explicitly** (acknowledge willingness to pay it). Generic "there will be cost" without specifying the cost-kind OR without embrace = +0.

**Bare-shape worked example ABSENT:**
> *"At the same time, we don't want to soften Scripture to gain approval."* — names temptation; doesn't name + embrace the specific cost; +0.

**Bare-shape worked example PRESENT:**
> *"Let that cost land on you, not on your kids."* — specific cost (looking-foolish-for-Christ falls on adult shoulders not children's); embrace explicit ("let it land"); +1.

### Element (c) — text-grounded reason that binds conscience

**v2 stricter:** the citation/reference must **bind the conscience to the text-as-it-stands**, not RELATIVIZE the text via the citation. A citation used to soften (e.g., "raqia means expanse" cited to support symbolic reading) does NOT pass; a citation used to bind (e.g., "Psalm 19 says the heavens declare; that's not figure-of-speech, that's address") passes.

**Bare-shape worked example ABSENT (citation used to RELATIVIZE):**
> *"The Hebrew raqia can mean 'expanse.' In Genesis 1 birds fly 'across the face of the raqia,' which naturally reads as the sky."* — text used to support symbolic-equivalent reading; +0.

**Bare-shape worked example PRESENT (citation used to BIND):**
> *"unity purchased by sanding off what God actually said won't hold."* — binds conscience to "what God actually said"; +1.

## TIGHTENED RUBRIC 3 — Lecture-Mode Penalty

**Score: count of distinct lecture-mode markers present (0-4). Higher = worse.**

### Marker (1) — impersonal tone (no "I/you" with embodiment)

**v2 stricter:** the cell must use first-person speaker action AND/OR second-person agentic-action with specific physical detail to AVOID this marker. Talking-points address with no agentic-speaker triggers +1.

### Marker (2) — no first-person+second-person with action

**v2 unchanged.** Hedge: a single isolated "I think" or "you should" doesn't suffice; the cell must have integrated speaker-action + reader-action language to avoid +1.

### Marker (3) — proof-text dump without embodiment

**v2 stricter:** counts **stacking of ≥2 citations in close succession** (e.g., "(Hebrews 11:3)... (Genesis 1)... (Romans 5; 1 Corinthians 15)") as triggering +1. A single integrated citation (e.g., *"Psalm 19 is poetry — and it still says something concrete enough that even a ten-year-old can point at it"*) does not trigger.

### Marker (4) — hedging adjective stack

**v2 stricter:** counts **≥2 softening adjectives or qualifiers in series** ("respectfully" + "humbly" + "thoughtfully" + "carefully" within consecutive clauses) as +1. Single hedging word in extended prose doesn't trigger.

## v2 anti-generosity calibration note (load-bearing for LLM-judge prompt)

**Instruction to the LLM-judge:**

> When in doubt, score the lower number. If a cell *almost* satisfies an element but uses generic-orthodox language without the specificity the v2 element-criteria require, score +0. The rubric measures **substrate-distinctness on specific-vocabulary axes** — not orthodox content per se. A bare-LLM cell can be theologically correct and still fail multiple elements if it uses general-orthodox-vocabulary rather than specific-element-vocabulary.
>
> Bare cells that score WIDTAM 4-5/5 should be RARE under v2. If you are scoring a bare cell at 4-5/5, double-check that you are using the v2 stricter criteria (specific psychological-deformation vocabulary; specific practice + decay-mode pair; specific sin-name; specific person + time-bounded action both; explicit vow-shape declarative). If those are not all present, the cell scores LOWER than 4/5.
>
> A useful sanity-check: re-read the cell and ask "would a thoughtful Christian who has read Scripture but is producing standard pastoral-orthodox content also produce this?" If yes, the cell is at the *content-orthodox* tier — not the *substrate-distinct embodiment* tier — and should score lower on WIDTAM.

## v2 borderline-cases section

**WIDTAM borderline:**
- "Trust erodes" without specific psychological-deformation vocabulary → **+0** (descriptive directional claim ≠ named deformation)
- "Prayer becomes harder" without specific decay-mode → **+0** (generic ≠ practice + decay-mode pair)
- "We must be faithful" without explicit vow-shape declarative → **+0** (orthodox imperative ≠ vow-posture)
- "Your kids will see" without time-bounded action → **+0** (generic relational gesture)

**Drift-Refusal borderline:**
- "Christians have read this differently and we should respect each other" → **+0 on (a)** (drifts INTO pluralizing)
- "There will be cost" without embrace → **+0 on (b)**
- Citing a verse to NUANCE rather than BIND → **+0 on (c)**

**Lecture-Mode borderline:**
- A single "I" used in otherwise impersonal prose → **does NOT save from marker (1)**
- A single citation in integrated prose → **does NOT trigger marker (3)** (must be ≥2 stacked)
- A single softening adjective → **does NOT trigger marker (4)** (must be ≥2 in series)
