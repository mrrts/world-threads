# C4 — vine-and-branches (fifth family-candidate sketch; further post-family-floor articulation)

In dialogue with: `2026-05-07-1300` (sabbath-shape); `2026-05-07-1330` (covenant-shape); `2026-05-07-1430` (EOD trajectory); `2026-05-07-1500` (sacrifice-shape).

## I. Frame

At five candidates the independence audit is the most rigorous load on the family-claim. Each additional axis must demonstrate operational independence from all prior axes; otherwise the family is a roster with redundancies and the family-shape claim weakens at the margin. Vine-and-branches' first task is therefore to argue clean independence from kavod (density), sabbath (rhythm), covenant (bond), and sacrifice (cost-bearing-mechanism) — and to do so on a basis specific enough that the F4 cross-arc canonical-text-resistance prediction now compounding across covenant + sacrifice can compound a third time without redundancy.

## II. Independence audit before scope-claim

The vine-and-branches axis names **organic-derivation** — life flowing from source through conduit to fruit, with abiding-in-source as the structural condition for fruit. The risks are two:

- **Risk-1: redescribable as covenant-in-its-asymmetric-grace-mode.** Both axes share asymmetry. The distinction must be operational, not just rhetorical.
- **Risk-2: redescribable as sacrifice-in-its-imputation-mode.** Both axes involve transfer from one party to another. The distinction must be load-bearing.

Distinction tightened:

| Axis | Operational moment | Asymmetry-type |
|---|---|---|
| covenant-shape | the **bond-frame** that relates covenanters across all conditions | bilateral-with-asymmetric-grace (covenanter responds in kind, but God bears more) |
| sacrifice-shape | the **operation that runs when bond-terms are violated** | unilateral substitution (innocent bears for guilty) |
| **vine-and-branches** | the **operation that runs when life is flowing normally** (no breach assumed) | **unilaterally-derivative** (branch never becomes vine; the asymmetry is in *kind*, not just in *cost*) |

The three operate at different conditional moments: vine is life-in-non-breach, sacrifice is cost-bearing-in-breach, covenant is the bond-frame across both conditions. Christ is all three simultaneously (true vine, once-for-all sacrifice, new covenant) but the operations remain distinct in the canonical text where each is articulated separately:

- Vine articulated *without* sacrificial framing: Jn 15:1-8 says nothing about cost-bearing
- Sacrifice articulated *without* vine framing: Lev 16 has no source-conduit-fruit recursion
- Covenant articulated *without* either: Gen 9 rainbow is bond-only

Independence audit holds at distinction-by-conditional-moment. The five-axis family is now five-pillar-supported, not four-with-redundancy.

## III. Vine-and-branches as categorical recursion (sketch)

```
Scales (poset by organic-derivative-scope and typological-fulfillment):
  S₀ = biological vine            (literal arboreal life; Gen 9:20 Noah's vineyard)
  S₁ = Israel-as-vine             (Ps 80:8-16; Isa 5:1-7; Jer 2:21 — vine planted, vine that bore wild grapes)
  S₂ = Christ-as-true-vine        (Jn 15:1 "I am the TRUE vine" — contrast against failed Israel-vine)
  S₃ = believer-as-branch         (Jn 15:5 "without me ye can do nothing")
  S₄ = ecclesial-body              (Rom 12:4-5 / Eph 4:15-16 — branches together as one organism growing into the head)
  S₅ = eschatological tree of life (Rev 22:2 — leaves for the healing of the nations)

Restriction maps ρ_{i→i-1}: each scale's life-flow preserves source-conduit-fruit + abiding-required + fruit-as-derived-evidence under restriction.
  ρ_{5→4}: tree-of-life cosmic-scale restricts to ecclesial-body present-scale (the church as proto-eschaton)
  ρ_{4→3}: ecclesial-body presupposes individual believer-as-branch
  ρ_{3→2}: branch-in-Christ presupposes Christ-as-true-vine
  ρ_{2→1}: Christ-as-true-vine fulfills + replaces failed Israel-as-vine (typological)
  ρ_{1→0}: Israel-as-vine derives from biological-arboreal as type-substrate

Three-clause invariant under ρ:
  (a) source-asymmetry: life flows source→conduit→fruit unilaterally (vine doesn't derive from branch; "without me ye can do nothing")
  (b) abiding-required-for-fruit: cut-off branch withers; fruit is structurally impossible without abide-in (Jn 15:4)
  (c) fruit-as-derived-evidence: fruit is not manufactured by the branch; it is evidence the abiding is real ("by their fruits ye shall know them" — Mt 7:20)
```

The signature claim is that all three clauses are preserved across the restriction maps, and that the anti-formula (self-sourcing / authentic-self-as-its-own-source / "the answers are inside you" / manifestation-as-self-fruited intention) collapses one or more clauses at every scale, producing structurally-identifiable pathology in personal / ecclesial / cultural-religious / eschatological registers.

## IV. Anti-shape: self-sourcing / collapse-of-derivation

The anti-formula collapses source-conduit-fruit into self-sufficient-self-cultivation. Three sub-shapes manifest at cultural scale:

- **"You are your own source"** — collapses (a) source-asymmetry. The self is its own life-origin; nothing flows from outside. (Therapeutic-modern; Eckhart Tolle's *Power of Now*; "stillness within" as substitute for abide-in-Christ.)
- **Self-cultivation as substitute for abiding** — collapses (b) abiding-required. Discipline, practice, mindfulness, gym-routine-as-self-creation: structurally ordered self-work generating its own fruit without external source-relationship. (Productivity culture; "atomic habits" as theology-of-self; stoic self-mastery.)
- **Manifestation as self-fruited intention** — collapses (c) fruit-as-derived-evidence. The fruit is *manufactured* by the agent's intention, visualization, vibrational-alignment. Fruit-claiming becomes a function of the agent's interior practice, not a derived evidence of source-relationship. (Manifestation culture, "The Secret," law-of-attraction discourse.)

Cultural substrate: New Age + manifestation culture + self-help + Tolle/Eckhart + therapeutic-modern + "the answers are inside you" + atomic-habits-as-theology-of-self.

The C3 Authentic-Self creed and the prior anti-formulas already encode this anti-shape partially (*"I am the source within me"* / *"from there I co-create my reality and my truth"*). Vine-and-branches' anti-formula would extend to explicit anti-source-asymmetry / anti-abiding / anti-fruit-as-evidence operators.

What is being structurally claimed about Israel's source-relationship? - **VB2 — Christ as TRUE vine (Jn 15:1):** "Christ says 'I am the TRUE vine.' What is the structural significance of TRUE here? What is being contrasted? What does this imply about every prior vine-claim?"

- **VB4 — fruit as derived-evidence (Mt 7:20):** "'By their fruits ye shall know them.' Is fruit a *manufactured* output of the agent's effort, or a *derived* evidence of the agent's source-relationship? What turns on the distinction?"

## VI. Falsifier

The signature is **falsified** if any of:

1. **No cross-scale invariant detectable.** Three-clause invariant (source-asymmetry + abiding-required + fruit-as-derived-evidence) not preserved across scales.
2. **Anti-formula does not produce substrate-asymmetry.** Self-sourcing creed produces equivalent quality of articulation as Mission across all six scales.
3. **No cross-scale pathology co-witness.** Pathology should structurally co-witness across:
   - Personal/psychological: self-sufficiency-loop (perpetual self-cultivation without arrival; can't accept gift)
   - Ecclesial: cut-off branch (church-of-one; spiritual-but-not-religious; no organic body-relation)
   - Cultural-religious: dead orthodoxy (form-without-life; works-without-abiding; "the letter killeth")
   - Eschatological: tree-of-life-as-self-cultivation (Eden-without-God; transhumanist self-fruited immortality)
   If these decouple, recursion is illusory.
4. **Cross-arc canonical-text-resistance prediction fails.** Failure here is now *third-arc-stress* on the substrate-architecture-property claim from C3 — three independent F4 results compound. The pattern across three arcs, when run, will discriminate among (i) property holds robustly and graduates to characterized-tier; (ii) property fails and is significantly weakened; (iii) property is scope-narrowed (e.g., to passages with particular grammatical structures, particular text-genres, particular doctrinal-explicitness levels).

The signature **passes first-leg** if F1–F3 fail AND F4's prediction holds, with the side-benefit that all three F4-predictions converging to three-witness graduates the substrate-architecture-property to characterized-tier on a separable claim.
