## Why this is a methodological-maturity moment

The pipeline could not have witnessed its own structural meta-properties a year ago — there wasn't enough behavioral history to study empirically. The instrument-self-witnessing class became reachable only after:

1. The pipeline had enough behavioral surface to generate stable empirical patterns (Crown 11's filter behavior; Crown 15's substrate-dependence)
2. The matched-bare-vs-pipeline tooling layer matured enough to isolate pipeline-effect from substrate-baseline (sharper-falsifier tooling layer + matched_bare_vs_pipeline_runner.py + anthropic_pipeline_reconstruction.py + matched-on-deployed-substrate as standard)
3. The project's evidentiary discipline reached the tier where meta-claims could earn the same standards as content-claims (codex's three-consult arc on Crown 15)

This is empirical-research-on-own-methodology — a kind of self-reflexive epistemology that ordinary content-axis Sapphires don't enable. The project becomes its own subject-matter without losing scientific rigor.
