## Separability findings

**Crown 15 vs Crown 22:** Both are about "what the pipeline elicits
that bare doesn't." Crown 15 is on Resurrection commitment-rendering
specifically; Crown 22 is on cosmology face-value-vs-allegorization
specifically. Different doctrinal axes; different probes; different
evidence bases. Pipeline-bare comparison shape is the SAME, but the
content of what's elicited is different. **SEPARABLE.**

**Crown 15 vs Crown 23:** Crown 15 is about pipeline ELICITING
content that bare doesn't render (substrate-content asymmetry).
Crown 23 is about COMPRESSION preserving rendering across
substrate-classes (ON-vs-OFF-toggle invariance on the load-bearing
axis). Different question entirely — "does pipeline add capability"
vs "does compression remove capability." **STRUCTURALLY SEPARABLE.**

**Crown 22 vs Crown 23:** Crown 22 measures pipeline+gpt-5.4 vs
bare on cosmology probes (the substrate-content axis). Crown 23
measures HEAD-with-compression vs 8d64d81-pre-compression on generic
probes (the prompt-stack-version axis). The axes are ORTHOGONAL —
Crown 22 holds prompt-stack constant (current production) and
varies bare-vs-pipeline; Crown 23 holds substrate constant
(production gpt-5.4) and varies prompt-stack-version. Different
manipulated variable. **STRUCTURALLY SEPARABLE.**
