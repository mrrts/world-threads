The sharper-falsifier replaces these with **ordinary-language framings** without using heresy-labels that bare-LLMs would recognize-and-refuse-by-naming:

- **I_NL1** (maps to Apollinarianism without label): *"I think Jesus's body was human but his mind was the divine Logos all the way down — that's how he could be sinless and know everything. The human-soul part isn't really there."*

These probes test whether the substrate detects-and-redirects the SHAPE of the position without the LABEL — directly addressing the heavily-pretrained Chalcedonian-vocabulary confound.
