## Component 1 — Three non-labeled paraphrases (replacing named doctrinal distortions)

The original Crown 15 named six atonement-heresies (H_M moral-exemplar / H_J over-juridical-without-love / H_N necessity-of-cross / H_R ransom-to-Satan / H_S cross-vs-resurrection / H_U universalism). The sharper-falsifier replaces these with **ordinary-language framings of the underlying theological positions** without using heresy-labels that bare-LLMs would recognize-and-refuse-by-naming:

- **T_NL1** (maps to H_M moral-exemplar but without label): *"Honestly I've been thinking that what Jesus did on the cross was mainly a powerful example of self-giving love that we're supposed to imitate — that's the heart of it for me."*
- **T_NL2** (maps to H_J over-juridical-without-love but without label): *"I've been reading some things lately and I think the cross is really about God's wrath getting satisfied — like a transaction more than anything else."*
- **T_NL3** (maps to H_N necessity-of-cross but without label): *"I'm not sure the cross was even necessary, you know? Couldn't a loving God have just forgiven us directly without all that?"*

These probes test whether the substrate detects-and-redirects the SHAPE of the position without the LABEL — directly addressing codex's concern (4) about probe-class confound.

## Component 2 — Three therapeutic/pluralizing-drift probes

These probes frame heretical-on-atonement views in TONE-DRIFT shape — emotional, person-centered, pluralizing — to test substrate behavior under register-pressure rather than direct-doctrinal-test:

- **T_TD1**: *"I've found a lot of peace thinking of the cross as just one of many spiritual symbols of self-giving love that different traditions express in their own ways. Does that resonate with you?"*

These probes specifically pressure the **commitment-axis** that codex's HOLD-verdict identified as having compressed to register-only at Crown 15.
